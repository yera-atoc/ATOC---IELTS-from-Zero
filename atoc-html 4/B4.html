📘 Grammar 1 — Passive Voice: основы и Present / Past Simple

💡 🔗 Связь с уровнем A: В A16 ты изучил базовый Passive (Present и Past Simple). Теперь расширяем: Passive во ВСЕХ временах + Impersonal Passive — ключевой инструмент академического письма в IELTS.

Пассивный залог используется, когда действие важнее, чем тот, кто его совершает. В науке и академических текстах это норма — мы фокусируемся на открытии, а не на учёном.

Формула Passive во всех временах

Время Активный залог Пассивный залог Формула
Present Simple Scientists conduct experiments. Experiments are conducted by scientists. am/is/are + V3
Past Simple They discovered penicillin in 1928. Penicillin was discovered in 1928. was/were + V3
Present Continuous Researchers are testing the vaccine. The vaccine is being tested. am/is/are + being + V3
Past Continuous They were building the lab. The lab was being built. was/were + being + V3
Future Simple Scientists will publish results. Results will be published. will + be + V3
Present Perfect They have completed the study. The study has been completed. have/has + been + V3
Past Perfect They had tested the compound. The compound had been tested. had + been + V3
Modal + V1 Scientists must review findings. Findings must be reviewed. modal + be + V3

Когда используем Passive?

✅ Используй Passive когда:

— исполнитель неизвестен

— исполнитель очевиден (учёные, все)

— фокус на результате/объекте

— академический/научный стиль

— официальные / юридические тексты

❌ Избегай Passive когда:

— текст становится слишком сухим

— агент важен для понимания

— неформальное общение

— личные истории и нарратив

— слишком много пассива подряд
❌ The experiment was conducted by the scientists in the laboratory. ✅ The experiment was conducted in the laboratory. (агент очевиден — убираем)
💡 В академических текстах by + агент часто убирают, если исполнитель очевиден (researchers, scientists, governments). Оставляй by-phrase только когда агент важен или неожиданен: 'The law was passed by a single vote.'

📝 Задание 1: Преобразуй в Passive Voice (убери by-phrase если не нужен)

▸ Scientists discovered a new vaccine in 2023.

▸ The government is funding three major research projects this year.

▸ Researchers have published the findings in Nature magazine.

▸ They will announce the results at the conference next month.

▸ The laboratory had tested over 500 compounds before the breakthrough.

📘 Grammar 2 — Perfect Passive и Modal Passive

Perfect Passive и Modal Passive — самые частые формы в академических текстах IELTS. Именно они придают тексту научный, объективный тон.

Present Perfect Passive: have/has been + V3

Структура Пример Когда использовать
have/has + been + V3 The data has been analysed by three independent teams. результат к настоящему моменту
have/has + been + V3 Several new treatments have been developed in recent years. недавние научные достижения
have/has not been + V3 The hypothesis has not yet been proven conclusively. незавершённые исследования

Past Perfect Passive: had been + V3

Структура Пример Когда использовать
had + been + V3 The compound had been tested extensively before approval. действие завершено ДО другого прошлого
had + been + V3 By 1950, the structure of DNA had been theorised but not proven. научная хронология
had not been + V3 The risks had not been fully assessed when trials began. упущения/недостатки в прошлом

Modal Passive: modal + be + V3

Modal Пример Значение
must be + V3 All results must be verified by peer review. обязательность
should be + V3 Data should be collected from multiple sources. рекомендация
can be + V3 This method can be applied across several disciplines. возможность
could be + V3 The findings could be interpreted in two different ways. предположение
may be + V3 New applications may be discovered through further research. вероятность
might be + V3 Additional funding might be required for phase two. слабая вероятность
❌ The results have been published by them last week. ✅ The results were published last week. (last week → Past Simple Passive)
💡 Present Perfect Passive + 'recently / in recent years / over the past decade' — идеальная комбинация для описания научного прогресса в IELTS Writing: 'Significant advances have been made in cancer treatment in recent years.'
💡 🎯 IELTS: Modal Passive — ключ к академическим рекомендациям в Task 2: 'Further research should be conducted... / Greater investment must be directed... / More rigorous testing could be introduced...' — эти конструкции дают Band 7.

📝 Задание 2: Заполни пропуски нужной формой Perfect / Modal Passive

▸ The new drug ___ (test) on over 1,000 patients before approval. (Present Perfect Passive)

▸ The experiment ___ (repeat) three times before the anomaly ___ (notice). (Past Perfect + Past Simple Passive)

▸ All clinical trials ___ (monitor) strictly by an independent committee. (Modal: must)

▸ The findings ___ (publish) in a peer-reviewed journal within six months. (Modal: should)

▸ By 2030, a cure for this condition ___ (develop) with current research funding. (Future Perfect Passive)

📘 Grammar 3 — Impersonal Passive: It is believed / argued / reported

Impersonal Passive — это особый вид пассива, который делает утверждение объективным и безличным. Он незаменим в академическом письме IELTS.

Две структуры Impersonal Passive

Структура Пример Примечание
It is + V3 + that-clause It is believed that climate change poses an existential risk. Самая частая форма в IELTS
It is + V3 + that-clause It is argued that renewable energy must replace fossil fuels. Для представления чужих взглядов
Subject + is/are + V3 + to-infinitive Climate change is believed to pose an existential risk. Более сжатая, элегантная форма
Subject + is/are + V3 + to-infinitive Renewable energy is argued to be the only viable solution. Band 7+ альтернатива

Глаголы Impersonal Passive — полный список

Глагол Перевод Пример
It is believed... считается, полагается It is believed that early intervention improves outcomes significantly.
It is argued... утверждается It is argued that privatisation has reduced the quality of public services.
It is claimed... заявляется It is claimed that the new treatment eliminates the virus entirely.
It is suggested... предполагается It is suggested that diet plays a greater role than genetics.
It is reported... сообщается It is reported that carbon emissions fell by 5% last year.
It is estimated... по оценкам It is estimated that 800 million people lack access to clean water.
It is widely accepted... широко признаётся It is widely accepted that vaccines are essential to public health.
It has been shown... было доказано It has been shown that exercise reduces the risk of chronic disease.
It has been found... было обнаружено It has been found that sleep deprivation impairs cognitive function.
❌ It is believed by scientists that the vaccine is effective. ✅ It is believed that the vaccine is effective. (by-phrase убирается в Impersonal Passive)

РАЗГОВОРНОЕ (не IELTS):

People think that science is important.

Many say that this drug works.

Experts believe plastic is harmful.

Some claim the study is flawed.

АКАДЕМИЧЕСКОЕ (IELTS Band 7):

It is widely accepted that science is fundamental to progress.

It is reported that this treatment produces significant results.

Plastic is believed to pose a serious environmental hazard.

The methodology of the study is claimed to be flawed.

📝 Задание 3: Перепиши, используя Impersonal Passive

▸ Scientists believe that gene editing could cure inherited diseases.

▸ Researchers have found that air pollution reduces life expectancy.

▸ People argue that universities should be free for all students.

▸ Experts estimate that 50% of species could be extinct by 2100.

▸ Many claim that social media negatively affects mental health.

📘 Grammar 4 — Academic Language: Passive в научных и IELTS текстах

Passive Voice — это не просто грамматика. Это стиль мышления академического автора: объективность, фокус на процессе, дистанция от «я».

Академические фразы с Passive для научных текстов

Функция Фраза (Passive) Контекст использования
Описание метода The experiment was conducted using a double-blind protocol. Task 1 / эссе о науке
Представление результатов Significant improvements were observed in the treatment group. Task 1 / описание данных
Ссылка на источник It has been demonstrated that antioxidants reduce cell damage. Task 2 / поддержка аргумента
Ограничения исследования The sample size was limited, so broader conclusions cannot be drawn. Task 2 / критическое мышление
Рекомендации Greater investment must be directed towards renewable energy research. Task 2 / заключение
Признание проблемы The issue has been widely acknowledged but insufficiently addressed. Task 2 / представление проблемы
Прогресс Remarkable advances have been achieved in the field of genomics. Task 2 / Body paragraph

Passive + Hedging: академическая осторожность

Сильное утверждение (опасно) Смягчённое (Passive + hedging) Уровень
Science has proved this is true. It has been suggested that this may be the case. Band 7
Everyone knows this drug works. This treatment has been reported to produce favourable outcomes. Band 7
This will definitely happen. It is widely anticipated that such developments will occur. Band 7+
💡 🎯 IELTS: Academic Upgrade: Комбинируй Impersonal Passive + hedging verb + that-clause для максимально академичного стиля. Пример Band 7+: 'It has been widely suggested that the accelerating pace of technological innovation may fundamentally alter the nature of employment within the coming decades.' — это звучит как настоящая академическая статья!

📝 Задание 4: Переведи в академический стиль, используя все инструменты B4

▸ Scientists are working on cancer cures. They have found some good results. (→ Passive + Impersonal Passive)

▸ The government should do more to fund research. This is very important. (→ Modal Passive + Impersonal)

▸ People think technology will change medicine completely. (→ It is widely... / believed to...)

▸ In the study, they tested the drug on 500 people and got positive results. (→ полностью академический Passive)

📄 Reading 1 — The Science of CRISPR

Прочитай текст. Найди и подчеркни все формы Passive Voice. Определи время каждой формы. Обрати внимание на случаи, когда by-phrase используется или намеренно опускается.

Few scientific developments have generated as much excitement — and controversy — as CRISPR-Cas9, a gene-editing technology that was first described in academic literature in 2012. Since then, its potential applications have been studied extensively across disciplines ranging from medicine to agriculture. It is estimated that over 10,000 scientific papers relating to CRISPR have been published in the past decade alone. The technology works by identifying specific sequences within DNA and cutting them with molecular precision. Faulty genes can be removed and, in some cases, replaced with healthy alternatives. It has been demonstrated in laboratory conditions that CRISPR can be used to eliminate genetic mutations associated with conditions such as sickle cell disease and certain cancers. However, the technology is not without controversy. It is argued by some ethicists that heritable genetic modifications — changes that can be passed to future generations — should not be permitted without greater societal debate. In 2018, a Chinese scientist was widely condemned after it was reported that he had used CRISPR to edit the embryos of human babies. As a result, stricter international guidelines have since been developed.

📄 Reading 2 — Space Exploration: Costs and Discoveries

Прочитай второй текст. Найди примеры Impersonal Passive (It is believed / argued / reported). Как они помогают автору сохранять академический тон и избегать категоричности?

Space exploration has long been regarded as one of humanity's greatest intellectual endeavours. Since the first artificial satellite was launched in 1957, hundreds of missions have been conducted, and vast quantities of scientific data have been collected. It is widely accepted that space research has produced technologies — including GPS, memory foam, and water purification systems — that are now used extensively in everyday life. Nevertheless, the cost of space programmes is frequently cited as a point of contention. It is argued by critics that the billions of dollars invested in space agencies could be more effectively directed towards addressing poverty, disease, and climate change on Earth. It is estimated that NASA's annual budget alone exceeds 25 billion dollars. Proponents counter that space exploration should not be viewed solely through an economic lens. It has been suggested that the discoveries made beyond Earth's atmosphere may ultimately be required to ensure the long-term survival of the human species. Whether this perspective is accepted or rejected, it cannot be denied that space science has fundamentally shaped our understanding of the universe.

В уроке B4 пишем эссе Task 2 на 170 слов. Фокус: максимально использовать Passive Voice и Impersonal Passive для создания объективного академического тона.

Чеклист Passive для Task 2

Место в эссе Рекомендуемая конструкция Пример
Introduction Impersonal Passive для парафраза It is widely argued that scientific research...
Body 1 — тема Present Perfect Passive для фактов Significant progress has been made in...
Body 1 — поддержка Modal Passive для рекомендаций Greater resources should be directed towards...
Body 2 — тема Impersonal Passive для другой точки зрения It is suggested by critics that...
Body 2 — поддержка Past Passive для исторических фактов When similar policies were introduced in the 1990s...
Conclusion Future Passive для прогноза Unless action is taken, the situation will be worsened...

📘 Writing Task 1 — Напиши эссе

Вопрос IELTS Task 2:

"Some people believe that governments should invest more in scientific research, while others argue that this money would be better spent on more immediate social needs. Discuss both views and give your own opinion."

Требования (170 слов):

▸ Introduction: Impersonal Passive для парафраза + thesis (своя позиция)

▸ Body 1: аргументы ЗА науку — Present Perfect Passive: 'remarkable discoveries have been made...'

▸ Body 2: аргументы ЗА социальные нужды — Modal Passive: 'resources must be directed...'

▸ Conclusion: Future Passive + финальная позиция

▸ Обязательно: минимум 3 разные формы Passive (Simple / Perfect / Modal / Impersonal)

💡 Band Check: 2 формы Passive правильно → Band 6. Добавь Impersonal Passive + Modal Passive → Band 7. Используй по 1 форме в каждом абзаце → Band 7+.

📗 Writing Task 2 — Образец эссе Band 7 с разбором

Образец (Band 7), ~170 слов:

It is widely debated whether public funds should be allocated primarily to scientific research or to addressing more immediate societal challenges. While both perspectives have merit, I believe a balanced approach must be adopted.

The case for increased scientific investment is compelling. Remarkable advances have been achieved through government-funded research, including the development of vaccines, renewable energy technologies, and life-saving medical treatments. It has been demonstrated repeatedly that discoveries made in fundamental research eventually produce practical applications that benefit entire societies. Furthermore, if adequate funding is not provided, critical areas of investigation may be abandoned entirely.

Nevertheless, it is argued by critics that pressing problems — poverty, inadequate healthcare, and deteriorating infrastructure — must be addressed as a priority. It is estimated that hundreds of millions of people currently lack access to basic services, and it is suggested that this suffering could be significantly alleviated through targeted government spending.

In conclusion, a false choice should not be presented between science and social welfare. Both must be adequately funded, as scientific progress is ultimately required if the most entrenched social problems are to be permanently resolved.

Разбор приёмов Band 7:

▸ Present Perfect Passive: 'have been achieved', 'has been demonstrated' — научные факты

▸ Modal Passive: 'must be adopted', 'must be adequately funded', 'could be alleviated'

▸ Impersonal Passive: 'It is argued', 'It is estimated', 'It is suggested' — объективность

▸ Future Passive в заключении: 'is not to be permanently resolved' — академический прогноз

▸ by-phrase используется только когда агент важен: 'by critics' — показывает источник оппозиции
