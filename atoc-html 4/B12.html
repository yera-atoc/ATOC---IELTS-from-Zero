Grammar 1 — Comparatives продвинутый: степени сравнения

В уровне A10 ты изучил comparatives и superlatives (bigger, the biggest, as big as). Сейчас мы переходим к продвинутым конструкциям: усиление, двойное сравнение, пропорциональное изменение — всё это необходимо для IELTS Band 7.

💡 Совет:

Связь с уровнем A: В A10 ты учил more expensive, the most expensive, as expensive as. Теперь добавляем: much more, far less, slightly higher, twice as many, the higher... the greater — академические конструкции.

Усиление степени сравнения (Intensifiers)

Интенсификатор Значение Пример (экономический контекст)
much / far / a lot намного GDP in developed nations is far higher than in emerging economies.
considerably / significantly значительно Inflation has risen considerably faster than wages this year.
slightly / marginally незначительно The unemployment rate is slightly lower than last quarter.
somewhat в некоторой степени Consumer confidence is somewhat stronger than analysts predicted.
a great deal очень намного Productivity in the technology sector has grown a great deal faster.
no нисколько не The revised forecast is no better than the original estimate.

Двойное сравнение: the + comparative... the + comparative

Структура Значение Пример
The higher X, the greater Y чем выше X, тем больше Y The higher the level of education, the greater the earning potential.
The more X, the less Y чем больше X, тем меньше Y The more automation increases, the fewer low-skilled jobs remain.
The faster X grows, the wider Y becomes proportional The faster the economy grows, the wider the wealth gap tends to become.

❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО:

The more higher the GDP, the more better living standards. (more + comparative — ДВОЙНАЯ ОШИБКА)

✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО:

The higher the GDP, the better the living standards. The more GDP grows, the better living standards become.

📝 IELTS подсказка:

В IELTS Task 1 (graphs/charts) конструкция 'the higher... the greater' помогает описывать корреляции между данными. В Task 2 она усиливает аргумент: 'The greater the investment in education, the more productive the workforce.'

Grammar 2 — Academic Quantifiers: выражение количества и пропорций

Academic Quantifiers — это точные слова для количества в академическом тексте. В IELTS Task 1 они критически важны для описания данных, а в Task 2 — для обобщений без чрезмерной категоричности.

Quantifiers: от большого к малому

Количество Quantifier Пример
почти все the vast majority of The vast majority of economists support free trade policies.
большинство the majority of / most The majority of developing nations rely heavily on commodity exports.
многие a significant number of / many A significant number of workers have been displaced by automation.
некоторые a proportion of / some A considerable proportion of government revenue comes from indirect taxes.
меньшинство a minority of Only a minority of small businesses benefit from trade agreements.
немногие few / a small number of Few developed nations have achieved truly equitable income distribution.
почти никто hardly any / very few Very few economies have managed to sustain growth above 5% for a decade.

Выражение пропорций и степени

Конструкция Пример
X times as many/much as The US economy is approximately 20 times as large as that of Poland.
twice / three times as + adj + as Average wages in Germany are roughly twice as high as in Portugal.
a + fraction + of Nearly a quarter of the global population lives on less than $2 a day.
approximately / roughly / around Approximately 60% of global trade involves manufactured goods.
over / more than More than half of the world's billionaires live in just three countries.
under / fewer than / less than Fewer than 10% of small firms export their products internationally.

❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО:

A majority of people thinks that... The most of economists agree...

✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО:

The majority of people think that... Most economists agree... / The majority of economists agree...

💡 Совет:

'Most' не требует 'the' когда употребляется без 'of': Most people agree... НО The majority of people agree... Ошибка 'the most of' — очень распространённая для студентов IELTS.

Grammar 3 — Comparing Data: язык для Task 1 и Task 2

IELTS Task 1 требует точного описания изменений, трендов и сравнений. Здесь объединяем comparatives, quantifiers и специальную лексику для описания графиков и диаграмм.

Описание изменений (Task 1 language)

Тренд Глаголы Наречия-модификаторы
Рост rise, increase, grow, climb, surge sharply, significantly, gradually, steadily, marginally
Падение fall, decrease, decline, drop, plummet sharply, dramatically, slightly, steadily, considerably
Без изменений remain stable, level off, plateau approximately, roughly, at around
Пик reach a peak, hit a high at its highest point in, peaking at
Минимум reach a low, bottom out at its lowest, falling to a low of

Сравнение двух показателей

Конструкция Пример из Task 1
X was considerably higher than Y GDP growth in China was considerably higher than in Brazil throughout the period.
X rose at a much faster rate than Y Exports rose at a much faster rate than imports between 2010 and 2020.
While X increased, Y declined While urban incomes rose steadily, rural wages remained largely stagnant.
X accounted for twice as much as Y Manufacturing accounted for roughly twice as much GDP as agriculture.
compared to / in comparison with Compared to the previous decade, productivity gains were far more modest.
in contrast to / unlike Unlike developed economies, emerging markets experienced stronger growth.

💡 Совет:

Для IELTS Task 1 никогда не пиши просто 'it went up'. Всегда: subject + verb + modifier + amount + time: 'GDP rose significantly by 3.2 percentage points between 2015 and 2020.' Это структура Band 7.

Grammar 4 — Academic Language: Comparatives в IELTS Task 2

Academic Upgrade: как использовать comparatives и quantifiers для построения убедительных аргументов в IELTS Task 2 — с конкретными примерами Band 7.

Academic Upgrade: Band 5 vs Band 7

Band 5 (слабо) Band 7 (академично) Улучшение
Rich countries have more money. The vast majority of global wealth is concentrated in a small number of highly developed economies. Quantifier + nominalization
Poor people have less opportunities. A disproportionate number of people in low-income nations have significantly fewer opportunities for social mobility. Quantifier + comparative + academic phrase
The economy is getting better and better. Economic indicators have improved considerably over the past decade, with GDP growing at a substantially faster rate than inflation. Comparative + data language
More and more people move to cities. An increasing proportion of the global population is migrating to urban centres, a trend that is considerably more pronounced in developing nations. Proportion + comparative + relative clause
Education is very important. A strong correlation exists between educational attainment and earning potential; the higher the level of education, the greater the likely income. The...the construction + academic tone

Сравнительные конструкции для аргументации

Функция Конструкция Пример
Усиление аргумента far more + adj + than Investment in education is far more effective than incarceration in reducing crime.
Пропорция a significantly greater proportion A significantly greater proportion of GDP is spent on healthcare in Scandinavian countries.
Парадокс despite being + comparative Despite being considerably wealthier, many developed nations have lower levels of reported well-being.
Тренд an increasingly + adj An increasingly unequal distribution of income poses a significant threat to social cohesion.

📝 IELTS подсказка:

Academic Upgrade: Экзаменаторы IELTS ищут 'Lexical Resource' — разнообразие слов. Не повторяй 'more' и 'less'. Чередуй: considerably higher / significantly lower / a great deal more / marginally fewer / substantially greater.

Reading 1 — Экономическое неравенство: данные и тренды

Прочитай текст. Найди и подчеркни все comparatives, superlatives и quantifiers. Запиши, какую функцию выполняет каждая конструкция.

Global economic inequality has widened considerably over the past four decades. The vast majority of economists agree that the gap between the wealthiest and poorest nations is now significantly larger than at any point in the post-war era. In the United States alone, the top 1% of earners now control a far greater share of national wealth than the bottom 50% combined. However, the picture is considerably more nuanced than these figures suggest. A significant proportion of people in East Asia have been lifted out of extreme poverty, largely due to China's remarkable economic transformation. The higher the level of integration into global markets, the faster economic growth has tended to be — though the distribution of those gains has been far from equal. In contrast, sub-Saharan Africa has experienced a much slower rate of development. Fewer than half of the countries in the region have achieved sustained GDP growth above 4% in recent years. A considerably greater proportion of government revenue is spent on debt repayment than on education or healthcare, perpetuating a cycle that is increasingly difficult to break.

💡 Совет:

В IELTS Reading задания на 'writer's view' часто связаны с comparatives. Слова 'considerably', 'far', 'significantly' перед comparative — это сигнал что автор делает strong claim. 'slightly' или 'marginally' = weak claim.

Reading 2 — Рынок труда: автоматизация и будущее работы

Прочитай текст. Выпиши 5 примеров использования quantifiers или comparatives и объясни их роль в аргументации автора.

The automation of labour markets represents one of the most significant economic transformations of the twenty-first century. A growing proportion of tasks previously performed by human workers are now being completed by algorithms and robotic systems, a trend that is advancing at a considerably faster rate than most economists predicted a decade ago. The impact, however, is far from uniform. Highly skilled workers in knowledge-based industries have generally fared a great deal better than their counterparts in manufacturing and routine service roles. The higher the level of education and adaptability a worker possesses, the more resilient they tend to be in the face of technological disruption. Nevertheless, the vast majority of labour economists agree that automation will ultimately create as many new jobs as it displaces, though the transition period may be considerably more painful for certain segments of the workforce. Fewer than a quarter of displaced workers successfully retrain for emerging roles within two years, highlighting a significantly greater need for investment in lifelong learning and retraining programmes than currently exists in most national education systems.

💡 Совет:

Quantifiers в тексте часто содержат ключевую информацию для IELTS Reading. 'The vast majority' = почти все. 'Fewer than a quarter' = менее 25%. Умение быстро переводить quantifiers в проценты поможет в заданиях на matching и T/F/NG.

Writing Task 2 — 190 слов. Тема: Экономика и неравенство. Используй comparatives, quantifiers, the...the конструкции и академические фразы.

Writing Task 1 — Data Description Practice

Задание:

Опиши следующие данные в 3–4 предложениях (60–70 слов), используя язык сравнения:

Данные: В 2000 году уровень бедности в Стране A составлял 35%, а в Стране B — 18%. К 2020 году показатель Страны A снизился до 12%, а Страны B — до 9%.

Требования:

* Используй considerably / significantly / marginally для модификации comparatives

* Используй 'X times as high as' для прямого сравнения

* Используй While X declined, Y also fell (but more/less sharply)

* Band Check: точные данные + 3 comparative структуры = Band 6

Writing Task 2 — IELTS Essay (190 слов)

Тема эссе:

"The gap between the rich and the poor is growing in most countries. What are the causes of this trend, and what measures could be taken to address it?"

Структура (190 слов):

* Introduction (2 предл.): парафраз + план эссе

* Body 1 (4–5 предл.): причины неравенства (automation, globalisation, tax policy)

* Body 2 (4–5 предл.): меры решения (education investment, progressive taxation, welfare)

* Conclusion (2 предл.): summary + recommendation

Используй обязательно:

* Минимум 2 quantifiers (the vast majority / a significant proportion / fewer than)

* Минимум 2 comparatives с интенсификаторами (considerably higher / far greater)

* Конструкция the...the (The greater the inequality, the more unstable...)

* Минимум 2 номинализации (automation, distribution, inequality...)
