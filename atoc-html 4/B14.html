📘 Grammar 1 — Noun Clauses с «that»

В уровне A ты изучил придаточные времени и условия. Теперь мы рассматриваем Noun Clauses — придаточные предложения, которые выполняют роль существительного в предложении (подлежащее, дополнение, часть сказуемого).

Функция в предложении Пример Перевод
Подлежащее (subject) That education is vital is widely accepted. То, что образование важно, широко признано.
Дополнение (object) Researchers believe that access is unequal. Исследователи считают, что доступ неравномерен.
Именная часть сказуемого The problem is that funding has declined. Проблема в том, что финансирование снизилось.
После прилагательного It is essential that all children attend school. Важно, чтобы все дети посещали школу.
После существительного The fact that literacy rates vary is significant. Тот факт, что уровень грамотности различается, важен.

Ключевые глаголы, после которых идёт that-clause: believe, argue, suggest, claim, note, report, indicate, emphasise, acknowledge, demonstrate

Неправильно Правильно
The problem is education is expensive. The problem is that education is expensive.
💡 В академическом письме слово 'that' после глагола часто можно опустить в речи, НО в IELTS Writing лучше оставлять его для ясности и формальности: 'Research suggests that...'

IELTS подсказка

В IELTS Writing Task 2 используй that-clauses для представления фактов и мнений: 'It is widely acknowledged that quality education reduces poverty.' Это сигнализирует экзаменатору об академическом стиле (Band 6–7).

Задание 1

Соедини части предложений, добавив «that»:

• Many experts believe / digital learning will replace traditional schools

• The fact / dropout rates are high / concerns policymakers

• It is clear / investment in education pays off

• Studies have shown / early childhood education is most effective

• The issue is / not all students have internet access

📘 Grammar 2 — Noun Clauses с вопросительными словами (Wh-clauses)

Wh-clauses начинаются со слов what, who, where, when, why, how, whether/if и тоже выполняют роль существительного. Важно: в них используется прямой порядок слов (не вопросительный!).

Тип Пример (неправильно) Пример (правильно)
what I don't know what is the solution. I don't know what the solution is.
why She explained why did students fail. She explained why students failed.
how Research shows how does poverty affect learning. Research shows how poverty affects learning.
whether The question is whether should we reform. The question is whether we should reform.
who Nobody knows who is responsible. Nobody knows who is responsible. (✓)

Формула: Глагол + [wh-слово + подлежащее + сказуемое]

Функция Wh-clause Главное предложение
Дополнение what quality education means Experts debate what quality education means.
Подлежащее how resources are distributed How resources are distributed matters greatly.
После предлога about why the system is failing There is debate about why the system is failing.
С whether whether private schools are better The study examined whether private schools are better.
Неправильно Правильно
Nobody understands why is the gap so wide. Nobody understands why the gap is so wide.
💡 Запомни правило «возврата к нормальному порядку»: как только wh-слово становится частью придаточного (не вопроса), порядок слов меняется на прямой: Subject + Verb.

Задание 2

Исправь порядок слов в wh-clauses:

• Explain why is online education growing so fast.

• The report shows how do children learn better.

• Nobody can predict what will happen to universities.

• The question is whether should governments fund higher education.

• Describe how does the grading system work in your country.

📘 Grammar 3 — Сложные предложения: Adverbial Clauses

Adverbial Clauses — придаточные обстоятельства. Они отвечают на вопросы когда? почему? при каком условии? несмотря на что? В отличие от уровня A (основы), здесь мы изучаем более широкий спектр союзов для IELTS.

Тип придаточного Союзы Пример
Времени when, while, as soon as, by the time, until By the time students graduate, curricula have changed.
Причины because, since, as, given that Given that funding is limited, schools must prioritise.
Цели so that, in order that Governments invest in education so that citizens can compete.
Уступки although, even though, while, whereas Although literacy has improved, quality remains uneven.
Условия if, unless, provided that, as long as Students succeed provided that they receive proper support.
Сравнения as...as, just as Just as cities need infrastructure, nations need education.

Позиция придаточного в предложении

Позиция Пример Запятая?
В начале (fronted) Although funding has increased, outcomes remain poor. Да, запятая обязательна
В конце Outcomes remain poor although funding has increased. Нет запятой
Вставное The system, while improving, still has major flaws. Запятые с обеих сторон
Неправильно Правильно
Although the system is good but results are poor. Although the system is good, results are still poor.
💡 Никогда не используй 'although' и 'but' в одном предложении! Это одна из самых частых ошибок на IELTS. Выбери одно: 'Although X, Y.' ИЛИ 'X, but Y.'

IELTS подсказка

В IELTS Writing Band 7 требует разнообразия синтаксических структур. Используй adverbial clauses в начале предложений для академического стиля: 'Given that education shapes society, adequate funding is imperative.'

Задание 3

Объедини предложения с помощью нужного союза из скобок:

• Education is important. Resources are scarce. [although / because]

• Children learn better. They receive individual attention. [when / unless]

• The reform was implemented. Test scores continued to fall. [even though / so that]

• Schools need technology. Students can access digital resources. [in order that / whereas]

• Teachers are well-trained. Student performance improves. [as long as / since]

📘 Grammar 4 — Academic Language: Noun Clauses в IELTS Writing

Связь с уровнем A: В уровне A ты изучил Reported Speech (A18) и Linking Words (A19). Noun Clauses расширяют эти знания: теперь ты можешь строить сложные академические конструкции для Task 2.

🔗 Связь с уровнем A: В A18 ты использовал 'He said that...' — это простейший тип noun clause. В B14 ты применяешь эту структуру для академических reporting verbs и сложных аргументов в эссе.

Academic Phrases с Noun Clauses

Фраза Тип Использование в IELTS
It is widely argued that... that-clause после adj Вводная фраза для мнения большинства
Research demonstrates that... that-clause после глагола Ссылка на доказательства
What concerns many educators is... wh-clause как подлежащее Выделение проблемы
The evidence suggests that... that-clause Академическая аргументация
It remains unclear whether... whether-clause Признание неопределённости
What cannot be denied is that... структура с wh + that Сильное утверждение

Academic Upgrade: Band 6→7

Band 5–6 (простое) Band 6–7 (с noun clause)
Education is important. It is universally acknowledged that education shapes the future of societies.
We don't know the answer. What remains uncertain is whether current reforms will yield measurable improvements.
Money is the problem. The fundamental issue is that adequate funding has not been allocated equitably.
Students need support. Research consistently demonstrates that personalised support significantly enhances outcomes.

IELTS подсказка

Academic Upgrade: Используй структуру 'It is + adjective + that...' для введения аргументов. Академические прилагательные: widely acknowledged, generally accepted, broadly recognised, commonly understood, increasingly evident. Это покажет экзаменатору владение академическим регистром.

Задание 4

Перепиши предложения, используя академические noun clause конструкции:

• Many people think private schools are better. → It is widely argued that...

• We are not sure if online degrees have the same value. → What remains unclear is...

• The problem is the government doesn't invest enough. → The problem is that...

• Studies show early education helps. → Research demonstrates that...

• Equal access to education is very important. → It is broadly recognised that...

📄 Reading 1 — Образование и цифровые технологии

Прочитай текст. Найди и подчеркни все Noun Clauses (that-clauses и wh-clauses). Посчитай их — сколько всего?

Education systems around the world are undergoing profound transformation. What is evident is that traditional teaching methods, while still valued, are increasingly supplemented by digital technologies. Research demonstrates that students who have access to online resources perform better in standardised assessments, although the reasons for this are not entirely clear. Whether this improvement results from the technology itself or from the additional motivation it provides remains a subject of debate among educators. Given that internet access is unequally distributed, policymakers argue that governments must ensure that all schools are equipped with adequate infrastructure. The fact that educational inequality persists across socioeconomic groups is particularly concerning. It is widely acknowledged that quality education is the most reliable path out of poverty, yet many children in underfunded communities still lack access to even basic resources. What needs to happen is that national education budgets must be restructured so that disadvantaged schools receive proportionally greater support.

📄 Reading 2 — Должно ли высшее образование быть бесплатным?

Прочитай текст. Выпиши 5 noun clauses. Для каждого укажи: тип (that/wh/whether) и функцию в предложении (subject/object/complement).

The debate about whether higher education should be free is one that divides governments worldwide. Those who argue that university fees deter talented students from low-income backgrounds make a compelling case. What the evidence shows is that countries with free or subsidised higher education tend to have higher rates of social mobility. However, critics suggest that the assumption that free education automatically improves quality is flawed. The reality is that universities require substantial funding to maintain academic standards, and that this funding must come from somewhere. It is important to note that some of the world's highest-ranked universities operate in countries where tuition fees are charged. What seems clear is that the issue is not simply whether education should be free, but how educational systems can be designed so that financial barriers do not prevent talented individuals from reaching their potential. Addressing this will require that governments rethink how they allocate public resources.

Требования к абзацу:

• Используй минимум 2 noun clauses (that / wh / whether)

• Добавь один adverbial clause (although / given that / since)

• Используй академический словарь из Reading 1

Band Check: правильное использование noun clauses = Band 6.5

Writing Task 2 — IELTS Essay (~190 слов)

IELTS Writing Task 2 — Opinion Essay

Тема эссе:

"Some people believe that university education should be free for all students. Others argue that students should pay tuition fees. Discuss both views and give your own opinion."

Структура (~190 слов):

Introduction (2 предложения): Парафраз темы + Thesis statement

Body 1 (4–5 предложений): Аргументы ЗА бесплатное образование + пример

Body 2 (4–5 предложений): Аргументы ПРОТИВ + контраргумент

Conclusion (2 предложения): Итог + своё мнение

Обязательные конструкции:

• Минимум 3 that-clauses (It is argued that / Research shows that...)

• Минимум 1 wh-clause (What is clear is that... / What concerns many is...)

• Минимум 2 adverbial clauses (although / given that / whereas)

• Академические linking words: Furthermore / Nevertheless / In conclusion

Band Check: Использование noun clauses + сложных предложений → Band 6.5–7
