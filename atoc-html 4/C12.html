Noun Phrase Complexity

Где граница между информационной плотностью и нечитаемостью?

Тематика: Урбанизм · Словарь: C1–C2 Academic · Writing: Problem-Solution Essay

💥 Урочный крючок — Зачем это нужно?

Три способа назвать одно и то же явление:

Band 5: the problem of cities growing too fast

Band 7: the rapid and largely unplanned expansion of urban areas

Band 8: the largely unregulated spatial expansion of metropolitan peripheries driven by inadequate housing policy

Смысл один. Но Band 8 упаковывает в одно существительное всю причинно-следственную цепочку. Это не многословие — это плотность. И это именно то, что отличает академический текст от просто «сложного» английского.

📘 Grammar 1 · Pre-modification: стопка модификаторов перед существительным

Прилагательные, причастия и существительные-атрибуты в роли pre-modifiers
🟣

В уровне A ты ставил одно прилагательное перед существительным: 'a big city'.

В уровне B ты добавил второе: 'a rapidly growing urban population'.

В уровне C мы спрашиваем: сколько pre-modifiers можно поставить — и в каком порядке — прежде чем NP становится нечитаемым?
Тип pre-modifier Позиция в стопке Пример Примечание
Determiner / Quantifier 1-й (самый левый) the / this / several / such Всегда первый
Evaluative adjective 2-й inadequate, problematic, significant Субъективная оценка автора
Size / Age / Shape 3-й large-scale, long-term, rapid Объективные параметры
Origin / Material 4-й state-led, market-driven, technology-based Источник или материал
Noun as attributive modifier 5-й (перед головным сущ.) housing policy, urban governance, infrastructure deficit Существительное-атрибут
Head noun Последний expansion, framework, decline Главное слово NP

⚡ Практика: строим NP слой за слоем

Слой 1 — только head noun: expansion

Слой 2 — noun attributive: urban expansion

Слой 3 — participial: unplanned urban expansion

Слой 4 — evaluative: largely unplanned urban expansion

Слой 5 — size/scale: large-scale largely unplanned urban expansion

⚠ Слой 5 уже перегружен. Предел читаемости — 3–4 pre-modifiers. После этого нужно перенести информацию в post-modification.
Слабая NP (Band 5–6) Сильная NP (Band 7–8) Что добавлено
the growth of cities the accelerating spatial growth of post-industrial cities evaluative participial + noun attributive
problems with housing deep-seated affordable housing shortfalls stacked pre-modifiers: evaluative + compound attributive
a bad urban policy a demonstrably counterproductive urban densification policy adverb + participial + compound noun attributive
the increase in population the sustained demographic pressure on metropolitan infrastructure evaluative + noun-noun compound attributive

⭐ Model Sentence — Band 8

The largely market-driven, inadequately regulated spatial expansion of post-industrial metropolitan peripheries has generated a deeply entrenched affordable housing deficit that disproportionately burdens low-income urban residents.

① largely market-driven, inadequately regulated — два participial pre-modifiers через запятую: экономия без потери точности

② spatial expansion of post-industrial metropolitan peripheries — noun attributive chain: каждое слово добавляет семантический слой

③ deeply entrenched affordable housing deficit — стопка: adverb + participial + compound noun + head

④ that disproportionately burdens low-income urban residents — defining relative clause как post-modifier: агент последствия
❌ Типичные ошибки

Ошибка: The city's fast rapid uncontrolled growing population problem...

Правильно: The rapidly expanding, insufficiently managed urban population presents a significant governance challenge.

Почему: Нельзя ставить два прилагательных одного семантического класса подряд ('fast rapid'). Порядок pre-modifiers строго иерархичен

Ошибка: A big important urban housing policy failure

Правильно: A significant, systemic failure of urban housing policy

Почему: Более двух pre-modifiers без запятой между evaluative прилагательными — нечитаемо. Длинные цепочки лучше переносить в post-modification через 'of'
🔵 Examiner's Eye — Pre-modification

✔ 2–3 точных pre-modifiers → немедленный сигнал LR Band 7+: студент знает не только слова, но их синтаксическую роль

✔ Compound noun attributive ('housing deficit', 'infrastructure gap') → маркер академического регистра

✔ Evaluative participial ('deeply entrenched', 'largely unregulated') → демонстрирует авторскую позицию без 'I think'
⚠ Снижает балл: Стопка из 4+ pre-modifiers без post-modification — нечитаемо; экзаменатор останавливается и GRA снижается

📙 Grammar 2 · Post-modification: упаковка информации после существительного

Prepositional phrases · Relative clauses · Participial phrases · Appositive NPs · Infinitive phrases
🟣

Pre-modification — это сжатие.

Post-modification — это разворачивание.

В уровне C мы изучаем пять типов post-modifiers и учимся выбирать между ними в зависимости от того, какая информация нужна — ограничивающая, описательная или дополняющая.
Тип post-modifier Структура Функция Пример
Prepositional phrase (PP) Head + PP Ограничение / уточнение области The shortage of affordable housing in high-density urban corridors
Defining relative clause Head + who/which/that + clause Ограничение: только этот субтип Cities that lack integrated transport networks face disproportionate congestion costs.
Non-defining relative clause Head + , which/who , + clause Добавление факта без ограничения Megacities, which now house over half of humanity, face unprecedented governance challenges.
Reduced relative (participial) Head + V-ing / V-ed Компактная замена relative clause Policies designed to incentivise densification have produced mixed empirical results.
Appositive NP Head + , NP , Переименование / уточнение Urban sprawl, a phenomenon characterised by low-density outward expansion, has accelerated in the Global South.
Infinitive phrase Head + to-inf Цель или потенциал The capacity to absorb rural migration while maintaining service provision remains severely constrained.

📐 Критический выбор: defining vs non-defining relative clause

Defining (без запятых): ограничивает — говорит о конкретном подтипе:

'Cities that invest in public transport reduce congestion.'

→ Только те города, которые инвестируют. Остальные — нет.

Non-defining (с запятыми): добавляет факт — всё подлежащее уже определено:

'Tokyo, which has the world's most extensive metro network, offers a compelling model.'

→ Токио — уже конкретный город. Запятая говорит: 'кстати, вот дополнительный факт'.

⚠ Путаница defining/non-defining — одна из самых частых пунктуационных ошибок Band 6.

⭐ Model Sentence — Band 8

The governance deficit affecting rapidly expanding secondary cities — a structural failure driven by the mismatch between administrative capacity and demographic pressure — represents one of the most underappreciated obstacles to equitable urban development in the Global South.

① affecting rapidly expanding secondary cities — reduced participial post-modifier: компактнее, чем 'which affects rapidly expanding secondary cities'

② a structural failure driven by the mismatch — appositive NP + participial post-modifier: переименование + причина в одном блоке

③ between administrative capacity and demographic pressure — PP внутри appositive: два номинализованных NP в параллели

④ one of the most underappreciated obstacles to equitable urban development — superlative NP + PP chain: плотность без громоздкости
❌ Типичные ошибки

Ошибка: The cities which they don't have enough infrastructure they face problems.

Правильно: Cities lacking adequate infrastructure face a compounding set of governance and service-delivery challenges.

Почему: Двойное подлежащее ('the cities... they') недопустимо. Defining relative без запятых — никогда с запятой после head noun в ограничивающем значении

Ошибка: Urban sprawl, that has increased rapidly, is a problem.

Правильно: Urban sprawl, which has accelerated markedly in recent decades, presents a multidimensional planning challenge.

Почему: Non-defining relative всегда с 'which/who', никогда с 'that'. 'That' — только defining relative clauses
🔵 Examiner's Eye — Post-modification

✔ Reduced participial ('policies designed to...') вместо full relative clause → LR + GRA: экономность без потери смысла

✔ Appositive NP ('urban sprawl, a phenomenon characterised by...') → демонстрирует концептуальное владение темой (TA + LR)

✔ PP chain ('shortage of affordable housing in high-density corridors') → академическая точность без сложного синтаксиса
⚠ Снижает балл: Non-defining relative с 'that' вместо 'which' — систематическая пунктуационная ошибка, снижает GRA

📗 Grammar 3 · Of-genitive Chains & Nominalisation in NPs

Как «заморозить» глагол или целое событие в существительное — и зачем
🟣

В C2 мы изучали номинализацию как замену глагола на существительное.

В C12 мы изучаем, как номинализованные NP соединяются через 'of'-цепочки в сложные именные группы — и где это перестаёт работать.
Глагольная форма Номинализованная NP Of-genitive chain Академический эффект
Cities expanded unplanned the unplanned expansion of cities the largely unplanned spatial expansion of post-industrial cities Плотность + атрибуция + точность
Governments failed to regulate housing the failure of governments to regulate housing the systemic failure of municipal governments to regulate affordable housing provision Агент → of-genitive + infinitive post-mod
People migrate to cities the migration of people to cities the sustained rural-to-urban migration of economically marginalised populations Модификаторы описывают тип и агента
Infrastructure cannot cope the inability of infrastructure to cope the demonstrable inability of existing urban infrastructure to cope with accelerating demographic demand Evaluative + nominal subject + to-infinitive

⚠ Где of-цепочка становится нечитаемой

Допустимо (2 звена): the erosion of institutional trust

Нормально (3 звена): the erosion of institutional trust in democratic governance

Перегружено (4 звена): the erosion of the foundations of the legitimacy of the institutions of governance

Правило Band 8: не более 3 'of'-звеньев подряд. Если информации больше — разбей на два предложения или используй relative clause.

📐 Три функции номинализации в академическом тексте

1. Тематизация: номинализация позволяет сделать целое событие темой нового предложения.

'Cities expanded rapidly. This rapid expansion strained infrastructure.' — 'expansion' стало темой второго предложения.

2. Плотность: весь аргумент помещается в субъект предложения, освобождая предикат для нового утверждения.

3. Дистанцирование: 'the failure of policy' звучит объективнее, чем 'policymakers failed'.

⭐ Model Sentence — Band 8

The accelerating fragmentation of cohesive urban communities — a consequence of the spatially concentrated, tenure-differentiated provision of public housing — has undermined the social capital upon which effective neighbourhood-level governance critically depends.

① The accelerating fragmentation of cohesive urban communities — nominalised NP как субъект: 'communities fragment' → 'fragmentation of communities'

② a consequence of the spatially concentrated, tenure-differentiated provision of public housing — appositive + двойной participial pre-modifier + nominalised NP

③ the social capital upon which — PP-fronted relative clause (upon which = more formal than 'that... depends on')

④ effective neighbourhood-level governance critically depends — compound noun attributive + adverb before verb
❌ Типичные ошибки

Ошибка: The increasing of urbanisation is causing the rising of poverty.

Правильно: The acceleration of urbanisation is generating a corresponding intensification of urban poverty.

Почему: 'The increasing/rising of' — неправильная номинализация. Правильно: 'increase in' / 'rise in' / или полная номинализация: 'acceleration', 'intensification'

Ошибка: The failure of the solving of the problem of housing of cities

Правильно: The failure to address the urban housing crisis

Почему: Более трёх 'of'-звеньев подряд — нечитаемо. Используй infinitive post-modifier ('to address') и compound NP ('urban housing crisis')
🔵 Examiner's Eye — Nominalisation & Of-genitive

✔ Номинализация как субъект предложения → тематическая прогрессия из C9 + плотность NP = Band 8 CC + GRA

✔ 'Upon which / in which / through which' вместо 'that... on/in/through' → формальный регистр, повышает LR

✔ Точная номинализация ('intensification' vs 'increase in') → маркер C2 лексического контроля
⚠ Снижает балл: Номинализация без смысловой необходимости — текст становится тяжёлым и непрозрачным; читаемость страдает

📕 Grammar 4 · NP Architecture & Band 8 Upgrade

Полная система: pre + head + post + of-chain — как профессиональный автор строит именную группу
🟣

Теперь ты знаешь pre-modification, post-modification и of-genitive chains.

В Grammar 4 мы видим, как полная NP-архитектура работает в реальных предложениях Band 8 — и учимся проверять, не перешли ли мы границу читаемости.
Компонент NP Вопрос для проверки Если ответ 'нет' →
Pre-modifiers (≤3) Каждый ли добавляет новое семантическое измерение? Убери дублирующий или перенеси в PP
Head noun Это точная номинализация или общее слово ('problem', 'issue')? Замени на точную номинализацию ('deficit', 'fragmentation', 'mismatch')
Post-modifier (PP / rel / part) Ограничивает ли он NP или добавляет информацию? Выбрал ли ты правильный тип? Проверь: defining vs non-defining; full vs reduced relative
Of-genitive chain Не больше 3 звеньев? Можно ли заменить одно звено на compound noun? Разбей или замени: 'policy of the government' → 'government policy'
Вся NP целиком Можно ли прочитать вслух без паузы на понимание? Разбей на два предложения или используй appositive

📐 NP Anatomy: разбор образцового предложения

Предложение: The largely market-driven failure of post-war urban housing policy — a failure characterised by the systematic prioritisation of developer profitability over long-term community need — has produced an affordable housing deficit that disproportionately concentrates poverty in the urban periphery.

Субъект-NP: The largely market-driven failure of post-war urban housing policy

Pre: largely market-driven [evaluative participial]

Head: failure [точная номинализация]

Post 1: of post-war urban housing policy [PP: of-genitive, 3 звена]

Post 2: — a failure characterised by... — [appositive NP с participial]

Предикат: has produced an affordable housing deficit [nominalised NP как объект]

Post объекта: that disproportionately concentrates poverty in the urban periphery [defining relative]
🚀 Band 8 Upgrade

Band 6: Cities are growing fast and there is not enough housing for poor people.

Band 7: The rapid growth of cities has created a serious shortage of affordable housing, which particularly affects low-income residents.

Band 8: The largely unregulated spatial expansion of metropolitan areas — driven by structural misalignments between land-use policy and demographic demand — has generated a compounding affordable housing deficit that disproportionately concentrates socioeconomic vulnerability among newly arrived, low-income urban residents.
🔵 Examiner's Eye — NP Architecture

✔ Сложная субъект-NP с pre + head + post → первые 5 слов предложения уже сигнализируют Band 8 GRA

✔ Appositive NP как встроенный комментарий → показывает концептуальный анализ (TA) без лишнего предложения

✔ Compound noun attributive ('housing deficit', 'land-use policy') → маркер C2 академической лексики (LR)
⚠ Снижает балл: NP, которую невозможно прочитать без двойного перечитывания — снижает CC и GRA одновременно; читаемость важнее сложности

📖 Reading 1 · The Governance of Urban Expansion

Академическое чтение: NP-архитектура в урбанистическом дискурсе
📖 The Governance of Urban Expansion: Between Market Efficiency and Distributive Justice

The accelerating spatial concentration of economic activity within a small number of globally connected metropolitan nodes has generated a set of governance challenges whose complexity and scale have substantially outpaced the adaptive capacity of existing institutional frameworks. Of particular concern is the deepening mismatch between the territorial jurisdictions within which urban governments exercise authority and the functionally integrated economic regions — frequently spanning multiple administrative boundaries — within which the consequences of urban policy are actually felt.

More precisely, the governance deficit afflicting rapidly expanding secondary cities in the Global South represents a structurally distinct problem from that confronting the established megacities of the developed world. In the latter case, the primary challenge is one of institutional coordination across a fragmented but resource-rich governance landscape. In the former, it is the fundamental inadequacy of fiscal capacity — the inability of municipal governments to generate sufficient revenue to finance even basic infrastructure provision — that constitutes the binding constraint upon effective urban management.

What the comparative evidence therefore suggests is that no single model of urban governance is universally applicable. Notwithstanding the considerable influence exercised by international development organisations promoting market-oriented approaches to housing and land management, the empirical record of such approaches in contexts characterised by deep structural inequality is, at best, mixed. By extension, any credible urban governance reform agenda must begin not from abstract institutional blueprints but from a rigorous analysis of the specific configurations of political economy within which reform must operate.

📖 Reading 2 · Density, Equity, and the Politics of Urban Form

NP-архитектура в аргументации: от анализа к собственному эссе
📖 Density, Equity, and the Politics of Urban Form

The relationship between urban density and social equity is considerably more complex than the dominant narrative of contemporary urban planning discourse suggests. Proponents of high-density development — a constituency that encompasses environmental advocates, economic geographers, and an influential body of urban planning professionals — argue that the spatial compaction of urban populations reduces per-capita resource consumption, shortens commuting distances, and generates the agglomeration effects that drive productivity and innovation. These arguments are not without empirical foundation. Notwithstanding this, the equation of density with sustainability, and of sustainability with equity, reflects an analytical conflation that the evidence does not unambiguously support.

More precisely, it is the distribution of the benefits and burdens of densification — rather than the aggregate relationship between density and sustainability — that determines whether densification constitutes a genuinely equitable urban strategy. High-density development that displaces low-income residents to less accessible urban peripheries, substituting walkable mixed-use neighbourhoods with remote car-dependent settlements, may simultaneously increase metropolitan-level density while intensifying the spatial concentration of disadvantage at the neighbourhood level. In this respect, the currently dominant model of market-led densification represents not a solution to urban inequality but a spatial reconfiguration of its expression.

What is required, therefore, is a fundamental reorientation of the planning agenda — away from the aggregate metrics of density and sustainability and towards the granular question of who bears the costs and who captures the benefits of urban transformation. To this end, planning frameworks that mandate the cross-subsidisation of affordable housing within high-density developments, combined with robust protections for the tenure security of existing low-income residents, offer the most promising avenue for reconciling the efficiency imperatives of urban compaction with the distributive demands of spatial justice.

Задание:

The maps below show the layout of a coastal town in 1990 and 2020. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

📌 Model Opening — Band 8:

The most striking transformation evident in the comparison of the two maps is the large-scale conversion of formerly agricultural land on the eastern periphery into a high-density residential zone — a development that has been accompanied by a substantial expansion of the coastal road network and the construction of a mixed-use commercial district adjacent to the newly developed waterfront.

③ into a high-density residential zone — PP post-modifier specifying outcome

④ a development that has been accompanied by... — appositive NP + defining relative: adds the consequence without a new sentence

✍️ Writing Task 2 · Problem-Solution Essay (260 слов, цель: Band 7.5)

Задание:

Many cities around the world are experiencing serious problems as a result of rapid and largely unplanned urban expansion. What are the main problems caused by this phenomenon, and what measures could be taken to address them?

📌 Model Opening — Band 8:

The largely unregulated spatial expansion of urban areas — driven by the structural incapacity of existing planning frameworks to accommodate accelerating rates of rural-to-urban migration — has generated an interlocking set of governance, infrastructure, and social equity challenges whose collective severity demands a fundamental reorientation of urban policy.

① The largely unregulated spatial expansion of urban areas — full pre + head + post NP as subject: evaluative participial + compound noun attributive

② driven by the structural incapacity of existing planning frameworks — participial appositive: cause embedded without new sentence

③ to accommodate accelerating rates of rural-to-urban migration — infinitive post-modifier: what the incapacity fails to do

④ an interlocking set of governance, infrastructure, and social equity challenges — nominalised NP object: three-item list as post-modifier

👁 What the Examiner Sees:

TA (7–8): Проблемы и меры перечислены с достаточной конкретностью; позиция автора присутствует

CC (7–8): Nominalised NPs обеспечивают тематическую прогрессию между абзацами

LR (7–8): unregulated, incapacity, interlocking, reorientation — точная C2 лексика

GRA (7–8): Full NP architecture в субъекте + appositive + infinitive post-mod

📐 Структура эссе:

Intro (2 пр.): Complex subject-NP для проблемы → What-cleft для тезиса о мерах.

Problems (3–4 пр.): Каждая проблема = номинализованная NP как тема. Appositive для механизма.

💡 Central Question — Ответ:

Граница между информационной плотностью и нечитаемостью — не фиксированная линия, а вопрос читателя. Академический автор строит NP так, чтобы каждый компонент добавлял ровно одно новое семантическое измерение — и останавливается, прежде чем читатель должен перечитать.
