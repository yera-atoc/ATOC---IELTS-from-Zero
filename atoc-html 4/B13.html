Grammar 1 — Advanced Passive: обзор и расширение

В уровне A16 ты изучил Passive Voice в Present и Past Simple. Теперь мы осваиваем продвинутый Passive — во всех временах и в академических конструкциях, которые критически важны для IELTS Task 2 Band 7+.

💡 Совет:

Связь с уровнем A: В A16 ты учил 'is made / was discovered'. Теперь добавляем: has been proven, will be implemented, is being conducted, would have been prevented — Passive во всех временах и модальных формах.

Passive Voice во всех временах

Время Структура Пример (медицина)
Present Simple is/are + V3 Vaccines are administered to millions of people annually.
Past Simple was/were + V3 Penicillin was discovered by Alexander Fleming in 1928.
Present Continuous is/are being + V3 A new treatment for cancer is currently being tested.
Past Continuous was/were being + V3 The trial was being conducted when the results were challenged.
Present Perfect has/have been + V3 Significant advances have been made in gene therapy.
Past Perfect had been + V3 The drug had been approved before the side effects emerged.
Future Simple will be + V3 Universal healthcare will be introduced in the next decade.
Future Perfect will have been + V3 By 2030, a vaccine will have been developed for this disease.
Modal Passive modal + be + V3 Resources should be allocated more equitably across healthcare systems.
Modal Perfect Passive modal + have been + V3 More lives could have been saved with earlier intervention.

❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО:

The patient was being operated when the power failed. The drug has discovered in the 1990s.

✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО:

The patient was being operated on when the power failed. The drug was discovered in the 1990s.

📝 IELTS подсказка:

В IELTS Task 2 Passive Voice делает текст более объективным и академичным. Вместо 'doctors give patients medicine' пиши 'medication is administered to patients'. Это повышает формальность и Band Score.

Zadanie 1

Трансформируй предложения в Passive Voice, сохраняя время:

* Researchers are currently developing a new antibiotic. (Present Continuous Passive)

* The government has not funded this programme adequately. (Present Perfect Passive)

* Doctors could have prevented many deaths with better screening. (Modal Perfect Passive)

* Scientists will announce the results of the trial next year. (Future Simple Passive)

Grammar 2 — Impersonal Passive: It is believed / argued / suggested

Безличный пассив (Impersonal Passive) — одна из самых важных конструкций для IELTS Task 2. Он позволяет представлять аргументы без личной ответственности и создаёт академический, объективный тон.

Конструкции Impersonal Passive

Конструкция Значение Пример
It is believed that... считается, что... It is believed that universal healthcare leads to better population health outcomes.
It is argued that... утверждается, что... It is argued that privatisation of healthcare reduces overall quality of care.
It is suggested that... предполагается, что... It is suggested that lifestyle factors account for the majority of chronic diseases.
It is reported that... сообщается, что... It is reported that waiting times in public hospitals have increased significantly.
It is widely accepted that... широко признано, что... It is widely accepted that prevention is more cost-effective than treatment.
It has been shown that... было показано, что... It has been shown that early diagnosis dramatically improves survival rates.
It is estimated that... подсчитано, что... It is estimated that obesity costs national health systems billions annually.
It is generally recognised that... общепризнано, что... It is generally recognised that mental health has been underfunded for decades.

Альтернативная форма: Subject + is said/believed/reported to...

Impersonal (It is...) Personal subject form Значение
It is believed that the treatment works. The treatment is believed to work. считается, что лечение работает
It is reported that costs are rising. Costs are reported to be rising. сообщается о росте расходов
It is expected that the policy will change. The policy is expected to change. ожидается изменение политики
It has been shown that exercise helps. Exercise has been shown to help. доказано, что упражнения помогают

💡 Совет:

Обе формы правильны в IELTS. Чередуй их в эссе: один раз 'It is argued that...', другой раз 'Healthcare is widely believed to be...' — это демонстрирует Range of Structures и повышает Band Score.

Zadanie 2

Трансформируй предложения, используя Impersonal Passive:

* Many doctors believe that the healthcare system is underfunded. -> It is believed that...

* Researchers have shown that regular exercise reduces heart disease risk. -> It has been shown...

* People widely accept that smoking causes cancer. -> It is widely accepted...

* Experts estimate that 1 in 4 people will experience mental illness. -> It is estimated...

Grammar 3 — Passive с модальными глаголами: рекомендации и выводы

Сочетание Passive + Modal Verbs позволяет делать рекомендации, выводы и гипотезы в академическом стиле. Это ключевые конструкции для заключения и рекомендательной части IELTS Task 2.

Modal + Passive: настоящее и будущее

Конструкция Значение Медицинский пример
should be + V3 следует / нужно (рекомендация) Healthcare funding should be increased to meet growing demand.
must be + V3 необходимо (обязательство) Patient confidentiality must be maintained at all times.
could be + V3 можно / возможно (предложение) The burden on hospitals could be reduced through better preventive care.
might be + V3 возможно будет (осторожное предположение) A cure for Alzheimer's might be found within the next twenty years.
can be + V3 можно (возможность) Many chronic diseases can be prevented through dietary changes.
needs to be + V3 нуждается в (необходимость) The mental health system needs to be fundamentally restructured.
ought to be + V3 должно быть (моральное долженствование) Prescription drugs ought to be made affordable for all patients.

Modal Perfect Passive: выводы о прошлом

Конструкция Значение Пример
should have been + V3 должно было (но не было) The epidemic should have been contained at an earlier stage.
could have been + V3 могло бы быть (упущенная возможность) Thousands of deaths could have been prevented with better screening.
might have been + V3 возможно было The outbreak might have been caused by contaminated water supplies.
must have been + V3 наверняка было (дедукция) The spread must have been accelerated by inadequate quarantine measures.

❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО:

More money should invested in healthcare. The policy must been changed last year.

✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО:

More money should be invested in healthcare. The policy must have been changed last year.

Zadanie 3

Соедини части предложений, используя Modal Passive:

* preventive care / should / fund more generously -> Preventive care should...

* many hospital infections / could / prevent / with better hygiene -> ...

* the new drug / might / approve / by the end of the year -> ...

* resources / must / distribute / more equitably across regions -> ...

Grammar 4 — Academic Language: Advanced Passive в IELTS Task 2

Academic Upgrade: как использовать Advanced Passive для создания объективного, академического аргумента в IELTS Task 2 на тему здравоохранения.

Academic Upgrade: Band 5 vs Band 7

Band 5 (слабо) Band 7 (академично) Что улучшилось
Many people think healthcare is important. It is widely believed that access to quality healthcare is a fundamental human right. Impersonal Passive + academic noun phrase
The government should spend more money. A greater proportion of GDP should be allocated to public health infrastructure. Modal Passive + quantifier + nominalization
Doctors say smoking is very bad. It has been conclusively shown that tobacco consumption significantly increases the risk of cardiovascular disease. It has been shown + adverb + nominalization
We could have stopped the pandemic earlier. The pandemic could have been contained far more effectively had stricter protocols been implemented. Modal Perfect Passive + conditional inversion
People need to prevent diseases. A paradigm shift towards preventive medicine is urgently needed if the long-term sustainability of healthcare systems is to be ensured. Complex Passive + academic vocabulary

Ключевые фразы для Task 2 о здравоохранении

Функция Академическая фраза с Passive
Введение проблемы It is widely recognised that healthcare systems worldwide are facing unprecedented strain.
Представление аргумента It has been argued that the privatisation of healthcare leads to a two-tier system.
Рекомендация Greater emphasis should be placed on preventive medicine rather than reactive treatment.
Статистика It is estimated that non-communicable diseases account for approximately 70% of global deaths.
Вывод It can be concluded that a balanced approach, combining public and private provision, may be most effective.

📝 IELTS подсказка:

Academic Upgrade: В эссе на тему здравоохранения используй минимум 3 разных Impersonal Passive конструкции: It is believed / It has been shown / It is estimated. Это одновременно демонстрирует Range of Structures и делает аргументы убедительнее.

Zadanie 4

Улучши предложения до Band 7, используя Advanced Passive:

* Doctors think that mental health is ignored. -> It is widely believed that mental health...

* We need to give more money to rural healthcare. -> Greater resources should be allocated...

* Scientists proved that stress causes illness. -> It has been conclusively shown that...

* They could have prevented the outbreak. -> The outbreak could have been prevented...

Reading 1 — Системы здравоохранения: частное vs государственное

Прочитай текст. Найди и подчеркни все конструкции Passive Voice. Определи время и функцию каждой (описание факта, рекомендация, вывод).

Healthcare systems around the world are organised along a spectrum from fully publicly funded to largely privatised models. It is widely argued that each approach carries distinct advantages and disadvantages that cannot be easily reconciled. In countries where healthcare is primarily funded through taxation, such as the United Kingdom, services are generally provided free at the point of use. It has been shown that such systems tend to achieve more equitable health outcomes, as access is not determined by financial means. However, it is frequently reported that public systems are being overburdened by ageing populations and rising treatment costs, leading many to argue that greater private sector involvement should be considered. Conversely, in predominantly market-driven systems, it is argued that competition drives innovation and efficiency. Nevertheless, it has been documented that a significant proportion of the population may be excluded from adequate care if it cannot be afforded. It is therefore suggested that a hybrid model, in which core services are publicly guaranteed whilst additional provision is supplemented by private insurance, may represent the most sustainable solution.

💡 Совет:

В IELTS Reading конструкции It is argued / It is suggested сигнализируют о мнении, а не факте. В заданиях True/False/Not Given это важно: автор не утверждает как факт — он приводит точку зрения. Это частая ловушка!

Reading 2 — Психическое здоровье: глобальный кризис

Прочитай текст. Выпиши 5 конструкций Impersonal Passive (It is... / It has been...) и переформулируй каждую в личном стиле.

Mental health has long been regarded as the neglected dimension of global healthcare. It is estimated that approximately one in four people will experience a diagnosable mental health condition at some point in their lives, yet it is widely reported that mental health services receive a disproportionately small share of national healthcare budgets in most countries. The consequences of this underfunding are significant. It has been shown that untreated mental health conditions are strongly associated with reduced productivity, social isolation, and physical health deterioration. Furthermore, it is argued that the stigma surrounding mental illness must be actively dismantled if individuals are to be encouraged to seek help at an early stage. In recent years, the impact of social media on adolescent mental health has been widely debated. It has been suggested that excessive screen time is linked to increased rates of anxiety and depression, though it is acknowledged that the causal relationship has not yet been conclusively established. Greater investment in research and accessible mental health services should therefore be prioritised as a matter of public health urgency.

Writing Task 2 — IELTS Essay (190 слов)

Тема эссе:

"Some people believe that governments should provide free healthcare for all citizens, while others argue that individuals should be responsible for their own health. Discuss both views and give your own opinion."

Структура (190 слов):

* Introduction (2 предл.): парафраз + thesis statement

* Body 1 (4–5 предл.): аргументы ЗА государственное здравоохранение

* Body 2 (4–5 предл.): аргументы ЗА индивидуальную ответственность

* Conclusion (2 предл.): balanced view + final recommendation

Используй обязательно:

* Минимум 2 конструкции Impersonal Passive (It is believed / It has been shown)

* Минимум 2 Modal Passive (should be funded / needs to be addressed)

* Минимум 1 Modal Perfect Passive (could have been prevented)

* Академическая лексика: equitable / sustainable / provision / allocated
