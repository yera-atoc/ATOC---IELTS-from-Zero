📘 Grammar 1 — Active Participle Clauses: причастия на -ing

🔗 Связь с уровнем A: В A1–A5 ты изучал Present Continuous (is working) и герундий (enjoy working). Participle Clauses используют ту же форму -ing, но выполняют роль придаточного предложения — заменяют целое придаточное, делая текст компактнее и академичнее.

Active Participle Clause заменяет придаточное предложение, когда подлежащее главного и придаточного предложений совпадает. Форма: V-ing + остаток в начале или в конце предложения.

Придаточное (полная форма) Participle Clause (-ing) Значение
Because it recognised the opportunity, the firm expanded. Recognising the opportunity, the firm expanded. причина
While it was competing globally, the company cut costs. Competing globally, the company cut costs. одновременность
After it had secured funding, the startup launched. Having secured funding, the startup launched. предшествование
The manager, who was leading the project, resigned. The manager, leading the project, resigned. определение
The data showed growth, which confirmed the forecast. The data showed growth, confirming the forecast. результат/следствие

Позиция Participle Clause в предложении

Позиция Структура Пример
В начале (обстоятельство) V-ing + , + Main Clause Facing increasing competition, many firms downsize.
В конце (результат) Main Clause + , + V-ing The merger succeeded, generating significant returns.
В середине (определение) N + , + V-ing + , + V The CEO, believing in innovation, restructured the firm.
Having + V3 (предшествование) Having + V3 + , + Main Clause Having reviewed the data, analysts revised their forecasts.
Неправильно Правильно
Entering the market, profits increased rapidly. (разные подлежащие — dangling participle!) Entering the market, the company saw profits increase rapidly.
💡 Главная ошибка: Dangling Participle — причастный оборот «висит в воздухе», потому что подлежащее главного предложения не совпадает с подразумеваемым субъектом оборота. Всегда проверяй: кто совершает действие в -ing обороте?

IELTS подсказка

В IELTS Writing Band 7 причастные обороты в начале предложения — признак синтаксической гибкости. Используй их для: (1) описания причины действия; (2) описания одновременных процессов в бизнесе/экономике; (3) замены придаточных с 'which' для компактности.

Задание 1

Преобразуй придаточные предложения в Participle Clauses (-ing):

• Because it adopted a new strategy, the company recovered. → Adopting...

• While they were negotiating the contract, both parties made concessions. → Negotiating...

• The startup, which was disrupting the market, attracted investors. → The startup, disrupting...

• After they had analysed the data, the board made its decision. → Having analysed...

• The policy failed, and this led to significant financial losses. → The policy failed, leading...

📘 Grammar 2 — Passive Participle Clauses: причастия на -ed

Passive Participle Clauses используют форму V3 (Past Participle) и заменяют пассивные придаточные предложения. Подлежащее главного предложения является объектом действия в причастном обороте.

Пассивное придаточное Passive Participle Clause (-ed) Значение
Because it was founded in 1990, the firm is well-established. Founded in 1990, the firm is well-established. причина/предпосылка
The report, which was published last year, revealed key trends. The report, published last year, revealed key trends. определение
When it was merged with a rival, the brand lost its identity. Merged with a rival, the brand lost its identity. условие/обстоятельство
The strategy, which was designed by consultants, proved effective. The strategy, designed by consultants, proved effective. определение
Because it was listed on the stock exchange, the company gained visibility. Listed on the stock exchange, the company gained visibility. причина

Сравнение: -ing vs -ed Participle Clauses

Тип Форма Залог Пример
-ing (active) V-ing Активный — субъект совершает действие Launching the product, the firm gained market share.
-ed (passive) V3 Пассивный — субъект получает действие Launched in 2020, the product gained market share.
Having + V3 Having + V3 Активный, предшествующий Having launched the product, the firm reported growth.
Having been + V3 Having been + V3 Пассивный, предшествующий Having been acquired, the startup restructured its team.
Неправильно Правильно
Discussed at the meeting, the managers agreed on a plan. (менеджеры не обсуждались!) Discussed at the meeting, the plan was approved by managers.
💡 Проверка -ed оборота: замени его полным пассивным придаточным. 'Founded in 1990...' → 'Because it was founded in 1990...' Если замена звучит естественно — оборот правильный.

Задание 2

Преобразуй пассивные придаточные в -ed Participle Clauses:

• The strategy, which was approved by the board, was implemented immediately. → The strategy, approved...

• Because it was backed by investors, the startup scaled rapidly. → Backed...

• The product, which was designed for the Asian market, sold poorly in Europe. → The product, designed...

• After it was restructured, the company returned to profitability. → Restructured, the company...

• The report, which was commissioned by the CEO, identified three key risks. → The report, commissioned...

📘 Grammar 3 — Продвинутые применения Participle Clauses

Participle Clauses могут выражать различные смысловые отношения — причину, время, условие, уступку и сопутствующие обстоятельства. Контекст и запятые помогают понять значение.

Значение Полная форма Participle Clause Маркер
Причина Because the firm cut costs, it survived. Cutting costs, the firm survived. запятая в начале
Время (одновременно) While it was growing, the firm hired staff. Growing rapidly, the firm hired new staff. одновременность
Время (до) After it had completed the audit, the firm filed taxes. Having completed the audit, the firm filed taxes. Having + V3
Условие If it is managed well, risk can be minimised. Managed well, risk can be minimised. -ed в начале
Уступка Although it faced setbacks, the company persisted. Facing setbacks, the company persisted. (контекст) нейтральное -ing
Результат The merger succeeded, and this created synergies. The merger succeeded, creating synergies. запятая в конце

Participle Clauses с with (Absolute Participle)

Структура Пример Значение
With + N + -ing With profits rising, the firm invested in R&D. сопутствующее обстоятельство
With + N + -ed With debts repaid, the company could expand. завершённое состояние
With + N + adj With the market volatile, investors stayed cautious. обстоятельство + состояние
Неправильно Правильно
With increasing competition, many firms are closing. (правильно грамматически, но слабо академически) Faced with increasing competition, many firms have been forced to downsize. (сильнее и точнее)
💡 'With + N + -ing/ed' — это Absolute Participle. В академическом письме он добавляет контекст или фон для главного действия. Особенно полезен для описания экономических условий: 'With inflation rising, central banks have tightened monetary policy.'

IELTS подсказка

Для Band 7–8: используй разные типы participle clauses в одном эссе. Начни абзац с -ed (причина/условие), используй -ing в конце предложения (результат) и добавь 'with + N + -ing' для контекста. Это демонстрирует синтаксическое разнообразие, которое оценивается в Grammatical Range and Accuracy.

Задание 3

Объедини пары предложений, используя подходящий тип Participle Clause:

• The company faced a hostile takeover. It restructured its board urgently. → Facing...

• The new policy was implemented in January. It reduced operational costs by 15%. → Implemented..., it...

• The firm had secured three major contracts. It was able to expand its workforce. → Having secured...

• Profits were declining. The CEO decided to pivot the business model. → With profits...

• The brand was recognised globally. It commanded premium prices. → Recognised globally, the brand...

📘 Grammar 4 — Academic Language: Participle Clauses в IELTS Writing

🔗 Связь с уровнем A: В B4 (Passive Voice всех времён) и B7 (Reported Speech) ты уже работал со сложными глагольными формами. Participle Clauses объединяют активный и пассивный залог в компактные академические конструкции — идеал для IELTS Task 2.

Бизнес-тематика: ключевые Participle Clause паттерны

Паттерн Пример в бизнес-контексте Функция
V-ing + , + Main Clause Seeking greater efficiency, corporations increasingly adopt automation. причина действия
Having + V3 + , + Main Having dominated the market for decades, the firm faces new challengers. предшествующее событие
V3 + , + Main Clause Established in competitive markets, these firms demonstrate resilience. пассивная предпосылка
Main Clause + , + V-ing Global trade expanded in the 1990s, transforming supply chains worldwide. результат/следствие
With + N + V-ing With demand surging, manufacturers struggled to meet supply requirements. фоновые условия
Main + , + thereby + V-ing The firm reduced overheads, thereby improving its competitive position. намеренный результат

Academic Upgrade: Band 6 → Band 7

Band 5–6 (с придаточным) Band 7 (с Participle Clause)
Because it was driven by innovation, the company grew rapidly. Driven by a commitment to innovation, the company achieved rapid growth.
After they had identified the problem, managers revised the strategy. Having identified the structural problem, senior managers fundamentally revised their strategy.
The policy was introduced in 2015, and it reduced unemployment. Introduced in 2015, the employment policy successfully reduced unemployment by 8%.
While it was competing in global markets, the firm had to cut costs. Competing in increasingly saturated global markets, the firm was compelled to restructure its cost base.

IELTS подсказка

Academic Upgrade: Для IELTS Grammatical Range and Accuracy (Band 7) используй participle clauses для: (1) устранения повторяющихся подлежащих; (2) создания более плотного, информативного стиля; (3) демонстрации разнообразия — никогда не начинай два предложения подряд одинаковой структурой.

Задание 4

Перепиши абзац, заменяя минимум 3 придаточных предложения на Participle Clauses:

• Оригинал: 'Because it was founded in Silicon Valley, the tech company had access to talent. After it had raised initial funding, it hired 50 engineers. While it was developing its core product, it also built strategic partnerships. The product was launched in 2022, and it disrupted the market.'

• Подсказка 1: Founded in Silicon Valley, the tech company...

• Подсказка 2: Having raised initial funding, it hired...

• Подсказка 3: ...developing its core product, it also built...

• Подсказка 4: Launched in 2022, the product disrupted...

The global business landscape has undergone profound transformation over the past two decades, driven largely by rapid technological advancement and shifting consumer expectations. Recognising the potential of digital platforms, established corporations have increasingly invested in e-commerce infrastructure, thereby repositioning themselves in highly competitive markets. Having dominated traditional retail for generations, many legacy firms now face existential pressure from agile startups operating without the burden of physical storefronts. Faced with declining profit margins, several major retailers have restructured their operations, closing underperforming stores while simultaneously expanding their online presence. Backed by venture capital, new entrants have disrupted entire industries, forcing incumbents to innovate or risk obsolescence. The most successful companies, combining technological capability with deep market knowledge, have managed to adapt effectively. With consumer behaviour shifting irreversibly towards digital channels, businesses ignoring this trend risk significant market share losses. Having observed these patterns, analysts widely agree that digital transformation is no longer optional for firms seeking long-term sustainability.

📄 Reading 2 — Корпоративная социальная ответственность

Прочитай текст. Выпиши 5 Participle Clauses. Для каждого: (1) перепиши в виде полного придаточного предложения; (2) объясни, почему автор использовал причастный оборот (компактность / ритм / стиль).

Corporate social responsibility (CSR) has evolved from a peripheral concern into a central pillar of modern business strategy. Driven by growing consumer awareness and tightening regulatory frameworks, companies are increasingly expected to demonstrate measurable commitment to environmental and social goals. Having initially dismissed CSR as a cost burden, many corporations now recognise its strategic value in attracting both talent and investment. Research conducted across multiple industries suggests that firms integrating sustainability into their core operations consistently outperform those treating it as an afterthought. Facing pressure from institutional investors, large corporations have adopted detailed reporting frameworks, quantifying their environmental impact and disclosing governance practices. Listed on major stock exchanges, these firms are subject to rigorous scrutiny from analysts and shareholders alike. With regulation tightening globally, businesses failing to adapt their practices risk not only reputational damage but also significant financial penalties. Companies having embedded CSR into their culture, rather than treating it as a compliance exercise, demonstrate the strongest long-term results.

✍️ Writing Tasks — B16

Writing Task 1 — Sentence Transformation Practice

Задание: Перепиши абзац с Participle Clauses

Исходный абзац (Band 5–6 стиль):

"Many small businesses were affected by the pandemic. Because they had lost revenue, they were forced to cut staff. They also reduced their marketing budgets. After they had adapted to online sales, some businesses recovered. They used social media and digital advertising. This approach helped them to reach new customers."

Задача:

• Перепиши абзац, используя минимум 3 Participle Clauses

• Используй минимум один -ed оборот и один Having + V3

• Добавь 'thereby' или 'consequently' для логической связи

• Объём: ~70 слов

Band Check: замена 3+ придаточных на participle clauses = Band 6.5

Writing Task 2 — IELTS Essay (200–220 слов)

IELTS Writing Task 2 — Advantages/Disadvantages Essay

Тема эссе:

"Large multinational corporations have a significant impact on the countries in which they operate. Do the advantages of this outweigh the disadvantages?"

Структура (200–220 слов):

Introduction (2 предложения): -ed participle для контекста + thesis

Body 1 — Преимущества (5 предл.): -ing participle в topic sentence

Body 2 — Недостатки (5 предл.): Having + V3 или -ed в topic sentence

Conclusion (2 предл.): обобщение с participle clause

Обязательные конструкции:

• Минимум 3 Participle Clauses разных типов (-ing / -ed / Having+V3)

• Минимум 1 'With + N + -ing' для описания экономического контекста

• Минимум 1 ',thereby + -ing' для выражения намеренного результата

• Лексика из Reading: disrupt / restructure / outperform / embed

• Cleft sentence из B15: 'What multinationals bring is...' или 'It is...that'
