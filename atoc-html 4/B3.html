📘 Grammar 1 — Future Continuous: действие в процессе в будущем

💡 🔗 Связь с уровнем A: В A9 ты изучил will (базовое будущее) и going to (планы). В B2 — Past Continuous (действие в процессе в прошлом). Теперь соединяем эти идеи: Future Continuous (will be + V-ing) — действие, которое будет В ПРОЦЕССЕ в определённый момент будущего.

Future Continuous показывает: в какой-то момент в будущем действие уже будет идти. Мы «заглядываем» в будущее и видим процесс.

Структура предложения

Тип Структура Пример
Утвердительное Subject + will be + V-ing By 2050, the world's population will be approaching 10 billion.
Отрицательное Subject + will not be + V-ing Governments will not be ignoring demographic shifts for long.
Вопрос Will + Subject + be + V-ing? Will cities be expanding into rural areas by 2040?
Краткий ответ Yes, they will. / No, it won't. Yes, populations will be growing rapidly.

Когда используем Future Continuous?

Ситуация Пример Маркер
Действие в процессе в конкретный момент будущего At this time next year, researchers will be publishing new data. at this time next year / in 2050 / tomorrow at noon
Запланированный или ожидаемый процесс By the end of the century, cities will be managing larger populations. by the end of / throughout / over the coming decades
Вежливый вопрос о планах (нейтральный тон) Will you be attending the demographics conference? (нет особого маркера — интонация вежливая)
Прогнозируемая тенденция в будущем Developing nations will be experiencing rapid population growth. in the future / increasingly / over time
❌ By 2050, the population will growing to 10 billion. ✅ By 2050, the population will be growing towards 10 billion.
💡 Future Continuous vs will + V1: 'will grow' = факт/предсказание (результат), 'will be growing' = процесс в момент (длительность). В академическом прогнозе оба варианта допустимы, но Future Continuous звучит более научно!
💡 🎯 IELTS: В IELTS Writing Task 1, когда описываешь прогнозируемые тенденции на графике, используй FC: 'It is projected that urban populations will be increasing steadily throughout the coming decades.' — это Band 7 формулировка.

📘 Grammar 2 — Future Perfect: завершение к моменту в будущем

Future Perfect отвечает на вопрос: «К какому моменту в будущем действие уже закончится?» Структура: will + have + V3.

Структура предложения

Тип Структура Пример
Утвердительное Subject + will have + V3 By 2100, the global population will have reached 11 billion.
Отрицательное Subject + will not have + V3 Many nations will not have solved their ageing crisis by 2050.
Вопрос Will + Subject + have + V3? Will governments have introduced pension reforms by then?
Краткий ответ Yes, they will (have). / No, it won't (have). Yes, they will have. / No, they won't.

Ключевые временные маркеры Future Perfect

Маркер Значение Пример
by 2050 / by then к 2050 году / к тому моменту By 2050, the fertility rate will have dropped significantly.
by the time + Present Simple к тому времени, как... By the time this generation retires, pension systems will have changed.
in X years' time через X лет In fifty years' time, technology will have transformed healthcare.
before + момент до определённого момента Before 2060, many countries will have introduced migration reforms.
within the next X years в течение следующих X лет Within the next three decades, populations will have aged considerably.
❌ By 2050, the population will reached 10 billion. ✅ By 2050, the population will have reached 10 billion.
💡 Запомни формулу: BY + момент будущего → Future Perfect (will have + V3). Ошибка #1 — забыть HAVE: нельзя 'will reached', только 'will have reached'!

📘 Grammar 3 — Future Continuous vs Future Perfect vs Will: как выбрать?

Три будущих времени — три разных взгляда на будущее. Выбор зависит от того, что важно: процесс, факт или завершение.

Сравнительная таблица трёх времён

Время Структура Фокус Пример (тема: демография)
Will + V1 will grow Факт / предсказание / результат The global population will reach 10 billion.
Future Continuous will be growing Процесс в момент будущего In 2060, populations will be ageing rapidly.
Future Perfect will have grown Завершение К моменту By 2100, the population will have doubled.

Практика: одна ситуация — три времени

Контекст Предложение Время
Просто предсказание Developing countries will experience rapid population growth. Will + V1
В какой момент это будет идти? In 2045, developing countries will be experiencing rapid population growth. Future Continuous
К какому моменту завершится? By 2070, developing countries will have completed their demographic transition. Future Perfect

By the time — особая конструкция

💡 BY THE TIME + Present Simple → Future Perfect в главном предложении. Нельзя использовать Future после 'by the time'! 'By the time governments act, the crisis will have worsened.' — НЕ 'will worsen'.
Неправильно ❌ Правильно ✅
By the time they will solve this, it will be too late. By the time they solve this, it will be too late.
By 2050, the population will reached its peak. By 2050, the population will have reached its peak.
In 2060, we will completed the transition. In 2060, we will have completed the transition.

📘 Grammar 4 — Academic Language: прогнозы в демографии

Тема демографии — одна из самых частых в IELTS. Академические прогнозы требуют специальных глаголов проекции + будущих времён.

Глаголы проекции и прогноза (Projection Verbs)

Глагол Пример с Future Perfect / Continuous Уровень
project (прогнозировать) It is projected that the population will have grown by 20% by 2050. Band 6+
forecast (предсказывать) Demographers forecast that ageing will be accelerating throughout this century. Band 6+
predict (предсказывать) Scientists predict that fertility rates will have declined to 1.5 by 2060. Band 6
estimate (оценивать) It is estimated that 70% of people will be living in cities by 2050. Band 6+
anticipate (ожидать) Experts anticipate that healthcare costs will have doubled within 30 years. Band 7
expect (ожидать) It is expected that migration will be filling demographic gaps in Europe. Band 6

Academic Collocations: демография

Коллокация Перевод Пример
ageing population стареющее население An ageing population will be placing greater demands on healthcare.
demographic shift демографический сдвиг By 2060, a major demographic shift will have occurred in most regions.
fertility rate рождаемость The fertility rate will have fallen below replacement level by 2040.
life expectancy ожидаемая продолжительность жизни Life expectancy will be increasing in most developing nations.
dependency ratio коэффициент зависимости The dependency ratio will have risen sharply by the mid-century.
population decline убыль населения Several Eastern European countries will be experiencing population decline.
urbanisation rate темп урбанизации The urbanisation rate will have peaked in Asia by 2075.
💡 🎯 IELTS: Academic Upgrade: Используй конструкцию 'It is projected / estimated / anticipated that...' + Future Perfect/Continuous. Это делает текст объективным и академичным. Пример Band 7: 'It is projected that by 2050, ageing populations will have placed unprecedented pressure on pension systems across the developed world.'

📝 Задание 4: Составь академические прогнозы

▸ к 2060 году / мировое население / достичь пика → (It is projected / will have)

▸ к середине века / рождаемость / снизиться ниже уровня воспроизводства → (anticipate / will have)

▸ в 2040 году / системы здравоохранения / испытывать нагрузку → (estimate / will be)

▸ к 2070 году / большинство стран / завершить демографический переход → (forecast / will have)

📄 Reading 1 — An Ageing World

Прочитай текст. Найди и подчеркни все примеры Future Continuous и Future Perfect. Обрати внимание на глаголы проекции (is projected, is estimated и т.д.).

The world's population is ageing at an unprecedented rate. In 1950, only around 5% of the global population was aged over 65. By 2050, it is projected that this figure will have risen to approximately 16%, representing nearly 1.5 billion elderly individuals. By that point, many developed nations will already have been managing ageing populations for several decades. The consequences for public services will be profound. Healthcare systems will be treating significantly more patients with chronic conditions such as dementia and cardiovascular disease. It is anticipated that by 2060, governments will have spent trillions of additional dollars on pensions and elder care. Meanwhile, as younger populations shrink, the workforce will be contracting, and economies will be facing a growing dependency ratio. Some analysts suggest that automation and artificial intelligence will be compensating for labour shortages by the mid-century. It is estimated that by 2045, robots will have taken over many roles currently performed by human workers, potentially easing the economic burden of demographic ageing.

📄 Reading 2 — Migration and Demographic Balance

Прочитай второй текст. Определи: как автор использует Future Perfect и Future Continuous для построения аргумента? Найди минимум 3 примера.

As birth rates decline across the developed world, many governments are increasingly turning to migration as a solution to demographic imbalance. By 2050, it is forecast that countries such as Germany, Japan, and South Korea will have lost more than 10% of their current populations without significant immigration. Economic planners argue that by then, migrants will have become essential contributors to national workforces and pension systems. The picture in the developing world is strikingly different. Nations in sub-Saharan Africa will be experiencing rapid population growth throughout the coming decades. It is estimated that by 2100, Africa's population will have more than doubled, reaching approximately 4 billion people. This demographic divergence — ageing in the north, growth in the south — will be reshaping global migration patterns significantly. However, migration alone will not be solving all demographic challenges. Governments will also need to be investing in education and infrastructure to integrate newcomers effectively. By the time comprehensive policies are implemented, demographic pressures will have already intensified in many regions.

✍️ Writing Tasks — IELTS Task 2 (160 слов)

В уроке B3 пишем полноценное эссе Task 2 на 160 слов с использованием Future Perfect и Future Continuous для академических прогнозов.

Напоминание: структура эссе Task 2

Часть Объём Ключевые приёмы для B3
Introduction 2 предложения Парафраз + thesis. Используй projection verb: 'It is widely anticipated that...'
Body 1 4–5 предложений Topic sentence + Future Continuous (тенденция в процессе) + пример + вывод
Body 2 4–5 предложений Topic sentence + Future Perfect (завершение к дате) + пример + вывод
Conclusion 1–2 предложения Обобщение + финальный прогноз: by the time / will have

📘 Writing Task 1 — Напиши эссе

Вопрос IELTS Task 2:

"In many countries, the proportion of older people is increasing. What are the effects of this trend on society? What measures could be taken to address the challenges it creates?"

Требования (160 слов):

▸ Introduction: парафраз (используй 'demographic shift' / 'ageing population') + thesis

▸ Body 1: последствия — используй Future Continuous: 'healthcare systems will be facing...'

▸ Body 2: меры — используй Future Perfect: 'by 2050, governments will have introduced...'

▸ Conclusion: итог + прогноз с 'by the time'

▸ Обязательно: минимум 1 коллокация из Grammar 4 (dependency ratio / fertility rate / life expectancy)

💡 Band Check: Future Continuous + Future Perfect в одном эссе → Band 6. Добавь projection verbs (it is projected / anticipated) → Band 7.

📗 Writing Task 2 — Образец эссе Band 6–7 с разбором

Образец (Band 6–7), ~160 слов:

It is widely anticipated that demographic ageing will represent one of the most significant challenges of the coming century. This essay will examine the principal consequences of this trend and propose measures to mitigate its effects.

As the proportion of older citizens increases, healthcare systems will be managing far greater numbers of patients with chronic conditions, placing enormous strain on public finances. Simultaneously, the workforce will be contracting, and the dependency ratio will be rising as fewer working-age individuals support increasing numbers of retirees. Without intervention, this imbalance will fundamentally undermine economic stability.

To counter these pressures, governments must act decisively. It is projected that by 2050, nations that have invested in pension reform and skilled migration will have significantly alleviated demographic pressures. Furthermore, policies incentivising higher birth rates, such as subsidised childcare and parental leave, may help reverse declining fertility rates over the longer term.

In conclusion, by the time ageing populations peak globally, societies that have prepared comprehensively will have achieved a more sustainable demographic balance. Proactive policymaking today is therefore essential.

Разбор приёмов Band 7:

▸ Future Continuous: 'will be managing', 'will be contracting', 'will be rising' — тенденции в процессе

▸ Future Perfect: 'will have alleviated', 'will have achieved' — результат к дате

▸ Projection verb: 'It is projected that' — академический объективный тон

▸ Коллокации: 'dependency ratio', 'fertility rates', 'demographic pressures' — Band 7 лексика

▸ by the time + Future Perfect в заключении — сильная финальная конструкция
