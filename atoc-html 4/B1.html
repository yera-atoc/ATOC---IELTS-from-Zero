📘 Grammar 1 — Present Perfect Continuous: форма и значение

💡 🔗 Связь с уровнем A: В A11–A12 ты изучил Present Perfect (have/has + V3) и научился использовать for/since/ever/never. Теперь расширяем это: добавляем been + V-ing, чтобы показать, что действие ПРОДОЛЖАЕТСЯ или ТОЛЬКО ЧТО завершилось.

Present Perfect Continuous — это время, которое показывает: действие началось в прошлом и всё ещё продолжается, или только что завершилось, и мы видим его результат сейчас.

Структура предложения

Тип Структура Пример
Утвердительное Subject + have/has + been + V-ing She has been living in the city for 5 years.
Отрицательное Subject + have/has + not + been + V-ing They haven't been working on the project.
Вопрос Have/Has + Subject + been + V-ing? Have you been studying English long?
Краткий ответ Yes, I have. / No, she hasn't. Yes, he has been working here.

Когда используем have / has?

Местоимение Вспом. глагол Пример
I / You / We / They have been + V-ing They have been building new apartments.
He / She / It has been + V-ing The city has been growing rapidly.
❌ She has been live in London for 10 years. ✅ She has been living in London for 10 years.
💡 Запомни: после been всегда стоит глагол с -ing. Нельзя писать have been + V1 или have been + V3!

📘 Grammar 2 — For / Since / How Long с Present Perfect Continuous

Три ключевых слова — for, since, how long — самые частые маркеры этого времени в IELTS.

Маркер Значение Пример
for + период в течение (длительность) for 20 years / for a long time / for several decades
since + точка с момента (начало) since 2005 / since the industrial revolution / since childhood
how long? вопрос о длительности How long have people been moving to cities?
recently недавно, в последнее время Urban populations have recently been increasing rapidly.
lately в последнее время Governments have lately been investing in smart cities.

For vs Since — разница

FOR — длительность действия:

for 3 years

for a long time

for several decades

for the past century

for many generations

SINCE — момент начала:

since 1990

since the war ended

since she was born

since industrialisation

since the last election
❌ I have been living here since 3 years. ✅ I have been living here for 3 years.
💡 FOR + период (3 years, a long time), SINCE + момент (2010, last year). Если можно задать вопрос 'How long?' — используй FOR. Если 'Since when?' — используй SINCE.
💡 🎯 IELTS: В IELTS Writing можно написать: 'Urban populations have been growing rapidly for the past three decades.' Это звучит академично и профессионально!

▸ They have been building the metro ___ five years.

📘 Grammar 3 — Present Perfect vs Present Perfect Continuous

Оба времени используют have/has, но передают разные смыслы. Это частая ошибка на IELTS!

Время Фокус Пример
Present Perfect (V3) РЕЗУЛЬТАТ, факт I have written the essay. (эссе готово)
Present Perfect Cont. ПРОЦЕСС, длительность I have been writing the essay. (пишу сейчас/всё ещё)
Present Perfect (V3) Количество / сколько раз She has visited Paris three times.
Present Perfect Cont. Как долго / непрерывность She has been living in Paris for a year.

Тест: какое время подходит?

Предложение PPC ✓ PP ✓ Почему?
Cities ___ (grow) for decades. have been growing — процесс в течение времени
Scientists ___ (discover) a new solution. — have discovered результат / факт
People ___ (migrate) to urban areas since 1950. have been migrating — непрерывный процесс
The government ___ (build) 50 new schools. — has built количество, результат
❌ The population has been increased by 20%. ✅ The population has increased by 20%.
💡 Глаголы состояния (stative verbs) НЕ используются в Continuous! Нельзя: have been knowing / have been believing. Можно: have known / have believed.

Stative verbs — глаголы состояния (только PP, не PPC)

Категория Глаголы
Мышление know, believe, understand, think, realise, remember, forget
Чувства love, hate, like, prefer, want, need, wish
Восприятие see, hear, smell, taste, feel (мнение)
Состояние be, have (владеть), belong, contain, include, consist

📘 Grammar 4 — Academic Language: PPC в академических текстах

Present Perfect Continuous — мощный инструмент в академическом письме. Он показывает тенденции, процессы и изменения — именно то, что нужно в IELTS.

Academic Phrases с Present Perfect Continuous

Разговорное Академическое (IELTS Band 6–7)
Cities are getting bigger. Urban areas have been expanding at an unprecedented rate.
More people move to cities. The global population has been increasingly migrating to urban centres.
Things are changing a lot. Significant shifts have been occurring in demographic patterns.
Governments try to fix this. Authorities have been implementing a range of urban policies.
The problem has been going on. This issue has been persisting for several decades.

Discourse Markers для связи с этим временем

Маркер Функция Пример
As a result, следствие As a result, cities have been facing increasing pressure on infrastructure.
Furthermore, добавление Furthermore, rural areas have been experiencing population decline.
In recent years, временной маркер In recent years, the pace of urbanisation has been accelerating.
Over the past decade, период Over the past decade, governments have been investing in smart cities.
Consequently, последствие Consequently, housing prices have been rising in major cities.
💡 🎯 IELTS: Academic Upgrade: Используй PPC + over the past (decade/century/years) — это создаёт профессиональный академический стиль. Пример: 'Over the past century, the proportion of people living in cities has been rising steadily.' — Band 7 уровень!

📝 Задание 3: Перепиши академически

▸ Many people have been moving to big cities. → (используй: urban centres / rural areas / increasingly)

▸ The government has been trying to solve the problem. (→ authorities / address / urban challenges)

▸ Things have been getting worse for poor people. (→ socioeconomic conditions / deteriorating)

▸ Scientists have been studying this for 20 years. (→ researchers / investigating / phenomenon)

📄 Reading 1 — The Growth of Cities

Прочитай текст. Найди и подчеркни все глаголы в форме Present Perfect Continuous. Обрати внимание на маркеры времени (for, since, over the past).

Over the past century, cities around the world have been growing at a remarkable pace. In 1900, only about 15% of the global population lived in urban areas. Today, that figure has risen to more than 55%, and demographers have been predicting that this trend will continue. Developing countries, in particular, have been experiencing rapid urbanisation as people move from rural areas in search of better employment and education. Governments have been struggling to keep up with this demand. Cities have been expanding outwards, creating what urban planners call 'urban sprawl'. Infrastructure such as roads, schools, and hospitals has been facing increasing pressure. For the past two decades, authorities in cities like Mumbai, Lagos, and Jakarta have been investing heavily in public transport systems to reduce traffic congestion. However, the pace of development has not always matched the pace of population growth, and many urban residents have been living in overcrowded conditions for years.

📄 Reading 2 — Smart Cities and Urban Solutions

Прочитай второй текст. Найди примеры Present Perfect и Present Perfect Continuous. Определи: почему автор использовал то или иное время в каждом случае?

In response to the challenges of rapid urbanisation, many cities have been developing innovative solutions over the past decade. The concept of the 'smart city' has emerged as a popular framework for addressing these issues. Smart cities use technology and data to improve public services, and governments around the world have been adopting this model with growing enthusiasm. Singapore, for example, has been implementing its Smart Nation programme since 2014. The city-state has invested billions of dollars in digital infrastructure, and residents have been benefiting from services such as facial recognition transport systems and AI-powered waste management. Meanwhile, European cities like Amsterdam and Barcelona have been working on reducing carbon emissions by promoting cycling and renewable energy. Critics, however, have been arguing that smart city technologies create new forms of inequality, as not all residents have equal access to digital tools. The debate over the future of cities has been intensifying as populations continue to grow.

✍️ Writing Tasks — IELTS Task 2 (Введение в эссе)

В урок B1 мы начинаем изучение структуры IELTS Task 2. Фокус этого урока — научиться писать сильное Introduction (вступление) на 2 предложения: парафраз темы + thesis statement.

Структура Introduction для IELTS Task 2

Предложение Функция Пример
Sentence 1 Парафраз темы (перефразируй вопрос своими словами) In recent decades, cities worldwide have been growing at an unprecedented rate, raising important questions about sustainable development.
Sentence 2 Thesis Statement (твоя позиция или план эссе) This essay will argue that while urbanisation offers significant economic benefits, it also creates serious social and environmental challenges that governments must urgently address.

📘 Writing Task 1 — Introduction Practice

Вопрос IELTS Task 2:

"In many countries, people are moving from rural areas to cities. What are the causes of this trend? What problems does it create?"

Твоя задача — написать ТОЛЬКО Introduction (2 предложения):

▸ Sentence 1: перефразируй вопрос (не копируй слова из задания!)

▸ Sentence 2: напиши, что будет в твоём эссе (обозначь 2 аспекта: причины + проблемы)

💡 Band Check: Если ты перефразировал тему своими словами + написал чёткий thesis — это Band 6. Если использовал академическую лексику — Band 7!

📗 Writing Task 2 — Model Introduction Analysis

Образец Introduction (Band 7):

"Over recent decades, increasing numbers of people have been relocating from countryside regions to major urban centres, a phenomenon that has been accelerating across both developed and developing nations. This essay will examine the principal drivers of this migration trend and discuss the social, environmental, and infrastructural challenges that such rapid urbanisation inevitably generates."

Анализ — что делает это введение сильным:

▸ Использован PPC: 'have been relocating', 'has been accelerating' — показывает тенденцию

▸ Парафраз: 'countryside regions' вместо 'rural areas', 'urban centres' вместо 'cities'

▸ Thesis охватывает оба аспекта: причины (drivers) + проблемы (challenges)
