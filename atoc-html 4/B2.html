📘 Grammar 1 — Past Continuous: форма и значение

💡 🔗 Связь с уровнем A: В A7–A8 ты изучил Past Simple (worked / went) — завершённое действие в прошлом. Теперь расширяем: Past Continuous (was working / were going) показывает действие, которое было В ПРОЦЕССЕ в определённый момент прошлого.

Past Continuous описывает действие в процессе выполнения в конкретный момент или период в прошлом. Это «фоновое» действие.

Структура предложения

Тип Структура Пример
Утвердительное Subject + was/were + V-ing Scientists were developing new software in 2010.
Отрицательное Subject + was/were + not + V-ing The team wasn't working on AI at that time.
Вопрос Was/Were + Subject + V-ing? Were companies investing in tech back then?
Краткий ответ Yes, he was. / No, they weren't. Yes, they were. / No, it wasn't.

Was или Were?

Местоимение Вспом. глагол Пример
I / He / She / It was + V-ing She was coding when the power went out.
You / We / They were + V-ing They were testing the prototype all night.
❌ The engineers was developing a new app. ✅ The engineers were developing a new app.
💡 Запомни: с I/he/she/it → WAS, с you/we/they → WERE. Ошибка в was/were — одна из самых частых на IELTS!

Временные маркеры Past Continuous

Маркер Значение Пример
at that time в то время Researchers were at that time exploring machine learning.
at 9 o'clock last night в конкретный момент She was presenting her findings at 9 o'clock last night.
in 2005 / in the 1990s в период Companies were rapidly expanding in the 1990s.
all day / all week в течение всего дня/недели The team were working all week on the launch.
while (+ другое действие) пока, в то время как While he was coding, his colleague tested the system.

📘 Grammar 2 — Past Simple vs Past Continuous: while / when

Ключевое отличие: Past Simple — завершённое действие. Past Continuous — действие в процессе. Они часто используются вместе!

Схема взаимодействия

Ситуация Структура Пример
Длинное фоновое + короткое прерывающее was/were V-ing WHEN Past Simple Scientists were running tests WHEN the system crashed.
Два одновременных действия Past Continuous WHILE Past Continuous While she was coding, he was designing the interface.
Последовательность событий Past Simple AND Past Simple He finished the code and published the results.
Фон + конкретный момент Past Continuous AT THE TIME The team were developing the app. At that moment, the CEO arrived.

While vs When — разница

WHILE — длинное действие:

While + Past Continuous

→ фоновое действие

While engineers were testing the app,

a critical error appeared.

While she was presenting,

the projector stopped working.

WHEN — короткое/прерывающее:

When + Past Simple

→ прерывающее действие

Scientists were experimenting

when they discovered the solution.

The team was working

when the deadline was changed.
❌ While he worked, she was calling him. ✅ While he was working, she called him.
💡 Правило: WHILE + длинное (Continuous), WHEN + короткое (Simple). Но оба союза можно использовать с Past Continuous, если оба действия длинные: 'While/When he was coding, she was testing.'
💡 🎯 IELTS: В IELTS Writing Task 1 (описание графиков) используй Past Continuous для описания тенденций в процессе: 'While economic growth was declining in 2008, technology companies were simultaneously investing in new markets.'

▸ In 2010, social media ___ (begin) to influence consumer behaviour.

📘 Grammar 3 — Past Continuous: контекст, атмосфера и нарратив

Past Continuous — не только для прерывающих действий. Это время также создаёт контекст и атмосферу в академических и нарративных текстах.

Три функции Past Continuous

Функция Описание Пример
Фоновый контекст Что происходило вокруг события In the 1990s, the world was just beginning to connect to the internet when the dotcom boom started.
Незавершённость Действие было в процессе, но не завершилось The company was developing a revolutionary product, but it ran out of funding.
Параллельные действия Два процесса идут одновременно While tech companies were competing globally, governments were struggling to regulate the industry.

Past Continuous в академическом нарративе

Разговорное Академическое (IELTS Band 6–7)
People were starting to use the internet more. During this period, increasing numbers of individuals were beginning to adopt internet technologies.
Companies were trying to make money from tech. Corporations were actively seeking to monetise emerging digital platforms.
Scientists were looking for a solution. Researchers were simultaneously investigating multiple potential approaches to this technical challenge.
❌ While the experiment, researchers were noticing changes. ✅ While conducting the experiment, researchers noticed changes.
💡 В академическом тексте Past Continuous отлично работает с маркерами: 'during this period', 'at the time', 'simultaneously', 'throughout this decade' — они добавляют профессионализм!

📝 Задание 3: Создай фоновый контекст — допиши предложения

▸ In the early 2000s, technology companies ___ (transform) the global economy when...

▸ While governments ___ (debate) digital regulation, companies...

▸ Researchers ___ (investigate) artificial intelligence throughout the 1980s, but...

▸ The world ___ (not / pay attention) to cybersecurity when the first major attack...

📘 Grammar 4 — Academic Language: технологическая лексика IELTS

В темах технологий и инноваций IELTS использует специфическую лексику. Знание academic collocations с Past Continuous поднимает оценку до Band 7.

Ключевые коллокации: технологии и инновации

Коллокация Перевод Пример с Past Continuous
drive innovation стимулировать инновации Tech giants were driving innovation at an unprecedented pace.
disrupt the market нарушать рынок Start-ups were disrupting the market while established firms struggled.
leverage technology использовать технологии Companies were leveraging new technology to cut costs.
accelerate development ускорять развитие Investment was accelerating the development of AI tools.
reshape society преобразовывать общество Social media was gradually reshaping how people communicated.
adopt a framework принимать систему/подход Governments were adopting new regulatory frameworks for tech.
address challenges решать проблемы Researchers were addressing the ethical challenges of automation.

Discourse Markers для нарратива о прошлом

Маркер Функция Пример
At this juncture, в этот момент At this juncture, engineers were exploring entirely new approaches.
During this period, в этот период During this period, companies were rapidly expanding their digital presence.
Simultaneously, одновременно Simultaneously, regulators were attempting to keep pace with change.
In the meantime, тем временем In the meantime, smaller firms were developing niche solutions.
Throughout this era, на протяжении этой эпохи Throughout this era, society was adapting to digital transformation.
💡 🎯 IELTS: Academic Upgrade: Комбинируй Past Continuous + discourse marker + коллокацию для максимального эффекта. Пример: 'During this period, technology companies were actively leveraging artificial intelligence to reshape traditional industries.' — это Band 7+ структура!

📝 Задание 4: Используй коллокации — перепиши академически

▸ In 2010, many companies were trying to use new tech. → (leverage / emerging technologies)

▸ Some firms were changing how things worked. → (disrupt / established industries)

▸ At that time, scientists were trying to fix problems. → (address / technical challenges)

▸ The internet was making society different. → (reshape / global communication patterns)

📄 Reading 1 — The Digital Revolution

Прочитай текст. Подчеркни все примеры Past Continuous и Past Simple. Обрати внимание, как автор использует WHILE и WHEN для связи двух действий.

In the early 1990s, only a small number of researchers were using the internet for academic communication. While scientists at universities were sharing data through early network systems, most of the world was still relying on traditional methods of information exchange. Then, in 1991, Tim Berners-Lee introduced the World Wide Web, and everything changed. Throughout the 1990s, technology companies were racing to develop commercial applications of the web. When the dotcom bubble burst in 2000, many investors lost significant capital. However, while some companies were collapsing, others were quietly laying the foundations for what would become the modern digital economy. Amazon, Google, and other firms were refining their business models at exactly the time when the wider market was failing. These companies were not simply surviving the crisis — they were transforming it into an opportunity, and their innovations were shaping the technological landscape for decades to come.

📄 Reading 2 — Artificial Intelligence: From Research to Reality

Прочитай текст. Ответь на вопросы: 1) Что происходило в области ИИ в 1950-х? 2) Какие два параллельных процесса описывает автор в третьем абзаце?

For much of the twentieth century, artificial intelligence was considered a concept confined to science fiction. In the 1950s, while mathematicians were theorising about machine intelligence, the computational power required for practical AI simply did not exist. Alan Turing was developing his foundational ideas about machine computation at Cambridge, but the hardware to realise these concepts was decades away. By the 1980s, researchers were making significant progress. Companies were investing in 'expert systems' — programmes that could simulate human decision-making. However, when government funding was reduced in the late 1980s, the field entered what historians now call the 'AI winter'. While some laboratories were continuing their work quietly, public and commercial interest had largely disappeared. The revival came in the 2010s. While computing power was increasing exponentially, researchers were simultaneously accumulating the vast datasets needed to train neural networks. This convergence of conditions was setting the stage for the AI revolution that society is experiencing today.

✍️ Writing Tasks — IELTS Task 2 (160 слов)

В уроке B2 пишем полноценное эссе Task 2 на 160 слов. Структура: Introduction (2 предл.) + Body 1 (4–5 предл.) + Body 2 (4–5 предл.) + Conclusion (1–2 предл.).

Структура эссе IELTS Task 2 (160 слов)

Часть Объём Содержание
Introduction 2 предложения Парафраз темы + Thesis statement (твоя позиция)
Body Paragraph 1 4–5 предложений Topic sentence → развитие → конкретный пример → вывод
Body Paragraph 2 4–5 предложений Topic sentence → развитие → конкретный пример → вывод
Conclusion 1–2 предложения Парафраз тезиса + финальная мысль

📘 Writing Task 1 — Напиши эссе

Вопрос IELTS Task 2:

"Some people believe that technology has made our lives more complicated rather than easier. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"

Требования к эссе (160 слов):

▸ Introduction: перефразируй тему + напиши чёткую позицию (agree/disagree/partly agree)

▸ Body 1: ОДИН аргумент В ПОЛЬЗУ того, что технологии усложняют жизнь + пример

▸ Body 2: ОДИН аргумент В ПОЛЬЗУ того, что технологии упрощают жизнь + пример

▸ Conclusion: итог + финальная мысль

▸ Используй Past Continuous минимум 1 раз (для описания исторического контекста)

💡 Band Check: Используй 2–3 коллокации из Grammar 4 → Band 6–7. Добавь discourse markers (Furthermore, However, Consequently) → Band 7.

📗 Writing Task 2 — Образец эссе Band 6–7 (разбор)

Образец (Band 6–7), ~160 слов:

In recent decades, rapid technological advancement has prompted considerable debate about whether it has simplified or complicated daily life. While I acknowledge that technology introduces certain complexities, I fundamentally believe that its benefits substantially outweigh the drawbacks.

Admittedly, while earlier generations were navigating relatively straightforward communication methods, modern individuals must now manage an overwhelming array of devices, platforms, and digital obligations. This digital overload can generate stress and reduce genuine human interaction. For instance, professionals today are expected to remain contactable at all hours, a pressure that was simply inconceivable before smartphones existed.

Nevertheless, technology has fundamentally transformed access to information, healthcare, and opportunity. Medical researchers are now able to leverage artificial intelligence to diagnose diseases that were previously undetectable. Furthermore, remote work technologies have enabled millions to achieve a healthier work-life balance. These advances represent profound improvements in human welfare.

In conclusion, although technology can occasionally complicate modern life, its capacity to address fundamental human challenges makes it an overwhelmingly positive force. Societies must, however, develop frameworks to manage its less desirable consequences.

Анализ — ключевые приёмы Band 7:

▸ Past Continuous для контекста: 'were navigating', 'was inconceivable' — исторический фон

▸ Коллокации: 'leverage artificial intelligence', 'address challenges', 'develop frameworks'

▸ Discourse markers: Admittedly / Nevertheless / Furthermore / In conclusion

▸ Balanced argument: аргументы с обеих сторон → чёткая финальная позиция
