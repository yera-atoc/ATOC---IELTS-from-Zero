Урок C10 · Upper-Intermediate / Advanced

Causative & Advanced Passive

Кто действует — и почему иногда важно это скрыть?

Тематика: Биотехнологии · Словарь: C1–C2 Academic · Writing: Advantages/Disadvantages Essay

💥 Урочный крючок — Зачем это нужно?

Два предложения об одном и том же событии в биотехнологиях:

Band 6: Scientists edited the human genome to remove a genetic disease.

Band 8: The human genome was edited, with hereditary disease markers being effectively eliminated through CRISPR-based intervention.

Первое предложение показывает, кто действовал. Второе — умышленно скрывает агента, фокусируя внимание на процессе и результате. В биоэтике, праве и политике выбор между активным и пассивным залогом — это не грамматика. Это риторика.

📘 Grammar 1 · Causative Have & Get

Субъект организует действие — но не выполняет его сам
🟣

В уровне A ты изучал активный залог: 'I fixed the machine.'

В уровне B ты расширил это до passive: 'The machine was fixed.'

В уровне C мы спрашиваем: кто организовал действие, не выполняя его? И когда академический автор намеренно скрывает агента — почему?
Конструкция Формула Пример Оттенок значения
Have sth done S + have + O + past participle Researchers had the gene sequences analysed. Нейтральная организация; субъект — заказчик
Get sth done S + get + O + past participle The lab got the results verified independently. Более динамичный; намекает на усилие/настойчивость
Have sb do sth S + have + person + bare inf The institute had specialists review the protocol. Субъект поручает лицу конкретное действие
Get sb to do sth S + get + person + to-inf Regulators got companies to disclose trial data. Убеждение, давление или переговоры — не просто поручение
Have sth happen (unexpected) S + have + O + past participle The researcher had her findings challenged. Пассивное претерпевание — субъект не организовывал

⚡ Ключевое различие: Have vs Get в академическом контексте

В академическом письме 'have sth done' предпочтительнее 'get sth done' — 'get' воспринимается как разговорный регистр.

Исключение: 'get sb to do' естественен в академическом тексте, когда речь идёт о переговорах, согласовании или давлении:

✔ 'Policymakers have struggled to get pharmaceutical companies to comply with transparency requirements.'

⭐ Model Sentence — Band 8

Having had their methodological assumptions fundamentally challenged, the research team was compelled to have the entire dataset reanalysed by an independent consortium.

① Having had their methodological assumptions challenged — perfect causative participle clause: предшествующее событие

② fundamentally challenged — наречие усиливает причинно-следственную связь

③ was compelled to — пассивный модальный, подчёркивает внешнее давление (не собственное желание)

④ have the entire dataset reanalysed — causative have: организованное действие с постмодифицированным объектом
❌ Типичные ошибки

Ошибка: Scientists had the experiment failed due to contamination.

Правильно: Scientists had the experiment compromised by contamination. / The experiment was rendered invalid by contamination.

Почему: 'Had sth fail/happen' — нежелательное событие, но 'failed' некорректно здесь; causative + past participle требует правильного выбора причастия

Ошибка: The company got the drug approved by had external reviewers.

Правильно: The company had the drug approved by external reviewers.

Почему: Get + O + past participle — не добавляй 'by had'; агент вводится через 'by + NP' как в обычном passive
🔵 Examiner's Eye — Causative Structures

✔ 'Have sth done' в академическом контексте → немедленный сигнал GRA Band 7+

✔ Разграничение have/get → демонстрирует контроль над регистром (LR)

✔ Causative в participial clause ('Having had X reviewed...') → редкая сложная структура, повышает GRA до Band 8
⚠ Снижает балл: Смешение causative и обычного passive ('The results were had analysed') — грубая ошибка, резко снижает GRA

📙 Grammar 2 · Advanced Passive Structures

Impersonal passive · Double passive · Reporting passive · Modal passive
🟣

В уровне B ты использовал passive для изменения фокуса предложения.

В уровне C мы изучаем четыре продвинутых паттерна, каждый из которых выполняет свою риторическую функцию в академическом тексте.
Тип Формула Пример Функция
Impersonal passive It is + past participle + that-clause It is widely acknowledged that gene editing raises profound ethical questions. Дистанцирование от личного мнения; академическая объективность
Reporting passive S + is/are + reported/claimed/believed + to-inf CRISPR technology is considered to represent a paradigm shift in medicine. Хеджинг: атрибуция без конкретного источника
Double passive S + is + past participle + to have been + past participle The trial is known to have been discontinued following adverse findings. Два пассивных события: факт + его статус
Modal passive S + modal + be + past participle Such interventions should be subjected to rigorous ethical scrutiny. Рекомендация / необходимость без конкретного агента
Perfect passive S + has/have been + past participle The regulatory framework has been substantially revised in recent years. Результат прошлого действия; агент намеренно опущен

Impersonal Passive: когда использовать

Ключевые глаголы:

it is acknowledged / recognised / argued / suggested / noted / claimed / contended / assumed / accepted / established that...

Эффект: автор представляет взгляд как разделяемый сообществом, не называя конкретного источника. Это стандартная академическая техника хеджинга.

Reporting Passive: ключевые глаголы

Группа 1 — факты:

is known / is shown / is found / is demonstrated to...

Группа 2 — мнения:

is believed / is considered / is thought / is reported / is alleged to...

⭐ Model Sentence — Band 8

It is widely contended that germline editing ought not to be permitted until the long-term consequences of heritable modifications have been more thoroughly investigated, a position that is considered to reflect the emerging consensus among bioethicists.

① It is widely contended that — impersonal passive с hedging adverb; дистанцирование + широкое приписывание

② ought not to be permitted — modal passive с отрицанием; рекомендательная сила без личного агента

③ have been more thoroughly investigated — perfect passive в subordinate clause; результативная незавершённость

④ is considered to reflect — reporting passive + complement; двойное хеджирование позиции
❌ Типичные ошибки

Ошибка: It is widely known that CRISPR has been discovered by Jennifer Doudna.

Правильно: CRISPR technology is widely attributed to the pioneering work of Jennifer Doudna and Emmanuelle Charpentier.

Почему: Impersonal passive с 'known that' не вводит новый факт с агентом; для атрибуции изобретения используй активный или 'attributed to'

Ошибка: The research is said to has been conducted.

Правильно: The research is said to have been conducted.

Почему: После reporting passive ('is said to') требуется perfect infinitive: 'to have been + past participle'
🔵 Examiner's Eye — Advanced Passive

✔ Impersonal passive ('It is widely acknowledged that') → мгновенный сигнал академического регистра (LR + GRA)

✔ Reporting passive + perfect infinitive ('is known to have been') → double passive = Band 8 структура

✔ Modal passive ('should be subjected to') → академически корректная форма рекомендации без 'I think'
⚠ Снижает балл: Passive везде без причины — текст теряет агентность и динамику; экзаменатор ценит осознанный выбор залога

📗 Grammar 3 · Agentless Passive: риторический выбор

Когда опускать агента — это стратегия, а не ошибка
🟣

Студенты часто думают: 'Я использую passive, потому что не знаю агента.'

Профессиональный автор думает: 'Я скрываю агента намеренно — чтобы создать определённый эффект.'

В уровне C мы изучаем пять риторических причин для выбора agentless passive.
Риторическая причина Эффект Пример Контекст
Процесс важнее агента Фокус на что произошло, не на кого Embryonic stem cells were extracted and cultured under controlled conditions. Научный метод / описание процедуры
Агент очевиден из контекста Избегание повторения субъекта The protocol was approved and subsequently implemented across all research sites. После упоминания агента
Намеренная деперсонализация Институциональный/юридический тон It has been determined that the compound poses unacceptable risks. Корпоративные / официальные решения
Политическая осторожность Скрытие ответственности Mistakes were made in the management of the clinical trial. Признание без принятия ответственности
Универсализация вывода От конкретного агента к общему принципу It can be concluded that gene therapy holds significant therapeutic promise. Выводы / заключения эссе

🔍 Критический взгляд: passive как сокрытие ответственности

Знаменитый пример — политический дискурс: 'Mistakes were made.' Эта фраза признаёт факт ошибки, но полностью уклоняется от указания виновного. В биоэтике и корпоративных отчётах agentless passive используется так же.

Сравните:

Active: 'The pharmaceutical company concealed adverse trial data from regulators.'

Agentless passive: 'Adverse trial data were not disclosed to regulatory authorities.'

Факт тот же. Но второе предложение не называет виновника. Академический читатель должен замечать эту разницу — и уметь её воспроизводить с осознанием.

⭐ Model Sentence — Band 8

It has been acknowledged, albeit belatedly, that insufficient safeguards were implemented during the early phases of human trials, a failing that has since been attributed to systemic shortcomings in regulatory oversight rather than to individual malfeasance.

① It has been acknowledged — impersonal perfect passive: институциональное признание без агента

② albeit belatedly — concessive adverb встроен как критический комментарий автора

③ that insufficient safeguards were implemented — agentless passive: процесс без ответственного лица

④ has since been attributed to systemic shortcomings rather than to individual malfeasance — double passive + 'rather than' = снятие индивидуальной ответственности через системную интерпретацию
❌ Типичные ошибки

Ошибка: The data were collected by being analysed in the laboratory.

Правильно: The data were collected and subsequently analysed in the laboratory.

Почему: Passive нельзя строить через 'by being + V-ed' как основную связку двух действий; используй последовательные passive-клаузы

Ошибка: It was acknowledged by the committee that mistakes were done.

Правильно: It was acknowledged by the committee that errors had been made.

Почему: 'Mistakes were done' — неверная коллокация; 'mistakes/errors are made', не 'done'
🔵 Examiner's Eye — Agentless Passive

✔ Осознанный выбор agentless passive в нужном месте → демонстрирует зрелость автора (все 4 критерия)

✔ 'Has been attributed to' в академическом анализе → точная коллокация, повышает LR

✔ Комментарий к passive ('a failing that has since been...') → embedded relative = GRA Band 8
⚠ Снижает балл: Passive в нарративных абзацах там, где нужен активный агент — текст теряет ясность и снижает TA

📕 Grammar 4 · Интеграция и Band 8 Upgrade

Causative + Advanced Passive + Fronting: система, а не набор приёмов
🟣

Теперь ты знаешь causative, advanced passive и agentless passive по отдельности.

В Grammar 4 мы видим, как профессиональный автор объединяет эти инструменты в единую систему контроля над агентностью — кто действует, кто претерпевает, и что автор решает скрыть.
Ситуация Band 6 подход Band 8 подход Инструмент
Научный метод Scientists did experiments on cells. Cellular specimens were subjected to controlled experimental conditions. Agentless passive + nominalization
Институциональное решение The government stopped the research. The research programme was discontinued following an independent review commissioned by the regulatory authority. Passive + causative embedded
Признание с хеджингом Experts think this is dangerous. It is widely considered that such interventions pose risks that have yet to be adequately quantified. Impersonal passive + reporting passive
Рекомендация без 'I think' We should regulate this more. It is submitted that more rigorous oversight frameworks ought to be established through international consensus. Modal passive + impersonal passive
Критика без обвинения Companies hid the side effects. Adverse effects are reported to have been insufficiently disclosed to trial participants. Reporting + double passive

📐 Agency Control Matrix — выбор залога как решение

Перед каждым предложением в эссе профессиональный автор задаёт три вопроса:

1. Кто агент? — Нужно ли называть его?

2. Что важнее — процесс, результат или агент?

3. Какой эффект я хочу создать: объективность, критику, деперсонализацию или универсальный принцип?

Ответы на эти вопросы определяют выбор между: активным, causative, reporting passive, impersonal passive, agentless passive или double passive.
🚀 Band 8 Upgrade

Band 6: Scientists are doing experiments on human embryos, and this is a problem.

Band 7: Human embryonic research is being conducted, raising significant ethical concerns that have been widely documented in the scientific literature.

Band 8: It is widely contended that the ethical parameters governing human embryonic research have yet to be adequately established, a lacuna that is considered to reflect the absence of binding international consensus on the permissible boundaries of biotechnological intervention.
🔵 Examiner's Eye — Full Integration

✔ Смешение causative + advanced passive в одном абзаце → мощный сигнал GRA Band 8

✔ Agentless passive в описании + reporting passive в оценке → профессиональная дифференциация функций залога

✔ Modal passive для рекомендаций → корректная замена личного 'I think/we should'
⚠ Снижает балл: Все предложения в одном залоге (все active или все passive) — монотонность, снижает GRA и CC

📖 Reading 1 · The Ethics of Germline Editing

Академическое чтение: causative и advanced passive в научном дискурсе
📖 The Ethics of Germline Editing: Agency, Accountability, and the Limits of Consent

It is now widely acknowledged that the emergence of CRISPR-Cas9 technology has fundamentally altered the landscape of human genetics. What was once considered the exclusive province of speculative fiction has been rendered an immediate practical and ethical concern: the possibility of having heritable traits permanently altered in human embryos. The implications of such interventions are considered to extend not merely to the individuals so treated but to all subsequent generations — a population that has, by definition, been denied any opportunity to provide meaningful consent.

Of particular concern to bioethicists is the asymmetry between the pace of technological development and the rate at which ethical frameworks have been established. It is reported that regulatory bodies in several jurisdictions have had their oversight mechanisms substantially reformed in response to unsanctioned germline experiments that were conducted without adequate institutional approval — most notably, the case of the Chinese researcher who had embryos edited to confer resistance to HIV infection, a decision that was widely condemned and is now considered to represent a defining moment in the governance of human biotechnology.

What the foregoing analysis suggests is that the question is not whether germline editing will be permitted, but under what conditions it ought to be allowed to proceed. It is submitted by many within the field that such decisions should not be made unilaterally by individual nations but should be subjected to rigorous and binding international deliberation — a position that has been consistently advanced by the most respected voices in bioethics.

📖 Reading 2 · Corporate Responsibility in Biotechnology

Advanced passive в корпоративном и юридическом дискурсе
📖 Corporate Responsibility and the Governance of Biotechnological Risk

Among the most contested questions in contemporary bioethics is the degree to which private corporations should be held responsible for the downstream consequences of technologies they have developed and commercialised. It is frequently argued that existing liability frameworks are structurally ill-equipped to address harms that may not manifest for decades — or, in the case of heritable modifications, across multiple generations — by which time causal attribution will have been rendered exceedingly difficult by the passage of time and the complexity of intervening variables.

Of particular significance in this context is the practice of having early-stage risk assessments conducted by bodies that are financially dependent upon the companies under review — an arrangement that is widely considered to undermine the independence upon which meaningful oversight depends. It has been reported that several major biotechnology firms have had their internal ethics committees restructured following revelations that dissenting opinions had been systematically suppressed. In such cases, the passive voice of institutional reporting — 'concerns were noted', 'revisions were made' — has itself been identified as a linguistic mechanism through which accountability is diffused.

What the evidence therefore demands is not merely procedural reform but a fundamental reconceptualisation of how responsibility is to be assigned in an era in which the consequences of scientific decisions are neither spatially nor temporally bounded. It is submitted that liability frameworks ought to be redesigned to reflect the transnational and intergenerational nature of biotechnological risk — a position that has gained considerable traction in recent years but has yet to be translated into binding regulatory instruments.

📝 Задания к Reading 2 — Критический анализ

2. Автор цитирует: 'concerns were noted', 'revisions were made' — и называет это 'a linguistic mechanism through which accountability is diffused'. Объясните своими словами: как именно agentless passive 'диффундирует' ответственность? Приведите реальный пример из новостей или бизнеса.

3. Найдите double passive в тексте. Напишите его в активном залоге. Что делает double passive неудобным для перевода в активный — и почему это важно?

4. 'Has yet to be translated into binding regulatory instruments' — это отрицательный perfect passive с 'yet'. Найдите ещё одно такое предложение в тексте. Объясните: какую позицию автора выражает 'has yet to be done'?

5. Напишите абзац (70–80 слов) с продолжением текста, используя: (a) impersonal passive для введения аргумента, (b) causative have для описания необходимых институциональных изменений, (c) modal passive для финальной рекомендации.

✍️ Writing Task 1 · Mixed Data Description

Advanced passive в описании данных: процесс без агента
✍️ Writing Task 1 · Mixed Data (170 слов, цель: Band 7.5)

Задание:

The diagrams below show the process by which CRISPR gene-editing technology is applied in a laboratory setting, alongside a bar chart illustrating the number of approved gene therapy clinical trials in five countries between 2018 and 2023. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

📌 Model Opening — Band 8:

The process depicted involves a series of carefully sequenced stages, each of which must be completed before the subsequent phase can be initiated. What the accompanying data suggest is that while the underlying technology has been substantially refined, the rate at which clinical applications have been approved varies considerably across national regulatory contexts.

① The process depicted — reduced relative (= that is depicted); economy of expression

② each of which must be completed — modal passive embedded in relative clause = technical precision

③ has been substantially refined — perfect passive for process description; agent irrelevant

④ What the accompanying data suggest is — what-cleft linking process diagram to bar chart

👁 What the Examiner Sees:

TA (7–8): Описаны обе части данных + связь между ними через what-cleft

CC (7–8): Process description → passive throughout; comparison → active/passive mix

LR (7–8): sequenced, initiated, refined, regulatory contexts — точная техническая лексика

GRA (7–8): Modal passive + embedded relative + what-cleft в двух предложениях

📐 Структура эссе:

Overview (2 пр.): What-cleft для главного вывода. Agentless passive для описания процесса.

Process (3–4 пр.): Passive throughout: 'is inserted', 'is subjected to', 'are subsequently verified'.

Data (3–4 пр.): Mix: reporting passive ('is reported to have increased') + active для сравнения.

✍️ Writing Task 2 · Advantages / Disadvantages Essay

Causative и advanced passive в аргументации: от факта к позиции
✍️ Writing Task 2 · Advantages & Disadvantages Essay (270 слов, цель: Band 7.5)

Задание:

Some people believe that genetic engineering technologies such as CRISPR should be made available for use in modifying human embryos to eliminate hereditary diseases. Others are concerned about the risks and ethical implications of such interventions. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of allowing germline editing in humans, and give your own opinion.

📌 Model Opening — Band 8:

It is widely acknowledged that the prospect of having hereditary diseases effectively eliminated through germline modification represents one of the most significant therapeutic advances to have been made available to medical science in recent decades. Nevertheless, it is equally contended that the risks attendant upon such interventions — particularly the potential for heritable off-target mutations to be inadvertently introduced — have yet to be adequately quantified.

① It is widely acknowledged that — impersonal passive: дистанцирование + широкая поддержка тезиса

② having hereditary diseases... eliminated — causative have gerund: процесс организован, не выполнен субъектом

③ to have been made available — reporting passive + perfect infinitive = double passive

④ have yet to be adequately quantified — negative perfect passive с 'yet' = хеджинг как аргумент против

👁 What the Examiner Sees:

TA (7–8): Advantages и disadvantages сбалансированы + чёткая собственная позиция в выводе

CC (7–8): Nevertheless как переход Adv→Disadv; impersonal passive создаёт академическую дистанцию

LR (7–8): attendant upon, inadvertently, quantified, heritable — C2 биоэтическая лексика

GRA (7–8): Causative gerund + reporting passive + double passive + 'yet to be' в одном параграфе

📐 Структура эссе:

Intro (2 пр.): Impersonal passive для контекста. Causative have для ключевого процесса.

Advantages (3–4 пр.): Agentless passive для описания медицинских процессов. Reporting passive для научного консенсуса.

Disadvantages (3–4 пр.): 'Have yet to be established'. Double passive для нераскрытых рисков. Modal passive для этических опасений.

Own Opinion + Conclusion (3 пр.): It is submitted that... ought to be subjected to... What the foregoing suggests is...

🎓 Урок C10 завершён!

✅ Causative have/get: субъект организует действие, не выполняя его — ключевое для академического и институционального дискурса

✅ Advanced Passive: impersonal, reporting, double, modal — четыре инструмента с разными риторическими функциями

✅ Agentless Passive как сознательный выбор: пять причин скрыть агента — от научной объективности до политической осторожности

✅ Agency Control Matrix: перед каждым предложением — осознанное решение о залоге

✅ Band 8 Integration: causative + advanced passive + fronting в единой системе академического письма

💡 Central Question — Ответ:

Кто действует — важный вопрос. Но иногда важнее вопрос: нужно ли читателю это знать? Академический автор уровня C управляет информацией об агенте так же осознанно, как управляет структурой предложения. Passive — это не отсутствие выбора. Это выбор скрыть.
