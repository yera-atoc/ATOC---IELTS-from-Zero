Сейчас мы расширяем это: ты изучишь полную систему discourse markers — слова и фразы, которые организуют академический текст, обозначают переходы между идеями и выстраивают логику абзаца. 📘 Grammar 1: Что такое Discourse Markers и зачем они нужны

Discourse markers (маркеры дискурса) — это слова и фразы, которые показывают логические связи между предложениями и абзацами. Они делают текст связным (coherent) и последовательным (cohesive) — два ключевых критерия IELTS Coherence & Cohesion.

Функция Discourse markers Пример в IELTS
Добавление идеи Furthermore, Moreover, In addition, Additionally Moreover, access to education remains severely limited for marginalised communities.
Противопоставление However, Nevertheless, Nonetheless, On the other hand However, critics argue that economic growth takes precedence over individual rights.
Уступка Although, While, Despite this, Even so, Admittedly Admittedly, progress has been made; nevertheless, systemic inequality persists.
Причина / следствие Therefore, Consequently, As a result, Hence, Thus Consequently, millions of people are denied access to basic healthcare.
Иллюстрация For instance, For example, To illustrate, Specifically For instance, the Universal Declaration of Human Rights enshrines 30 fundamental freedoms.
Обобщение / вывод In conclusion, Overall, To summarise, In short In conclusion, the protection of human rights requires coordinated international action.
Уточнение / акцент In particular, Notably, Crucially, Significantly Crucially, freedom of expression underpins all other democratic rights.
Последовательность First(ly), Second(ly), Subsequently, Finally Firstly, the right to education must be guaranteed; secondly, access must be equitable.
❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО ✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО
However I agree with this view, but there are problems. → (не ставь но после However — он уже является противопоставлением) However, I partially agree with this view, as there are several significant problems. → (запятая после However, далее без but)
💡 Лайфхак: Запомни правило пунктуации: discourse marker в начале предложения → ВСЕГДА запятая после него: "Furthermore, ...", "However, ...", "Consequently, ...". Исключение: в середине — тире или запятые с двух сторон: "This is, however, insufficient."

📝 Задание 1: Discourse Markers — выбери подходящий маркер из скобок и расставь запятые

▶ _____ (Furthermore / However / Therefore), the right to education is not universally enforced.

▶ Poverty limits access to justice. _____ (Admittedly / Consequently / For instance), legal representation remains unaffordable.

▶ The treaty was signed in 1948. _____ (Specifically / Nevertheless / In conclusion), violations continue today.

▶ Соедини два предложения, используя "Despite this" или "Nonetheless": "Governments promised reform. Discrimination persists."

▶ Напиши 2 предложения на тему прав человека, используя "Crucially" и "As a result".

📘 Grammar 2: Discourse Markers внутри предложения

Помимо discourse markers в начале предложения, существуют маркеры, которые работают внутри предложения — встраиваясь в структуру клаузы. Это позволяет создавать более сложные и элегантные конструкции.

Позиция Маркер Структура Пример
В середине however S + , however, + VP Governments, however, have failed to act.
В середине therefore S + , therefore, + VP Inequality, therefore, must be urgently addressed.
В середине nonetheless S + , nonetheless, + VP Progress, nonetheless, remains painfully slow.
Подчинительный although / while / whereas Although + clause, + main clause Although legislation exists, enforcement is weak.
Подчинительный despite / in spite of Despite + N/V-ing, + clause Despite ratifying the treaty, states fail to comply.
Относительный which — non-defining N, which + clause, The convention, which was adopted in 1989, protects children.

Сравнение: начало vs середина vs подчинение

НАЧАЛО предложения: СЕРЕДИНА предложения:
However, access to justice remains unequal. Access to justice, however, remains unequal.
Furthermore, women face systemic barriers. Women, furthermore, face systemic barriers.
Consequently, millions live in poverty. Millions, consequently, live in poverty.
Nevertheless, reform is possible. Reform, nevertheless, is possible.
❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО ✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО
Although the law exists, but enforcement is weak. → (although и but — дублируют функцию противопоставления) Although the law exists, enforcement remains weak. → (только одно подчинительное слово на одну связь)
💡 Лайфхак: Варьируй позицию discourse markers в своём эссе. Если в одном абзаце ты начал с "However,", в следующем используй "..., however, ..." в середине или "Although..." в начале. Это повышает оценку Coherence & Cohesion за разнообразие связей.
🎓 IELTS подсказка: Examiners specifically look for "a range of cohesive devices used accurately and appropriately" (Band 7). Это означает: не повторяй одни и те же маркеры, используй маркеры разных функций (не только "however" и "furthermore") и варьируй их позицию в предложении.

📝 Задание 2: Позиции discourse markers — перепиши каждое предложение, перенеся маркер в указанную позицию

▶ "However, the situation is improving." → поставь however в середину

▶ "Therefore, governments must act." → поставь therefore в середину

▶ "Governments have signed the treaty. Nevertheless, violations occur." → объедини с "Although"

📘 Grammar 3: Метод PEEL — структура академического абзаца

PEEL — это универсальный метод построения абзаца в академическом письме. Каждая буква — отдельный элемент: Point → Evidence → Explanation → Link.

Элемент Что делает Типичные discourse markers Объём
P — Point (Тезис) Формулирует главную идею абзаца — topic sentence One significant issue is... / It is widely argued that... / A key factor is... 1 предложение
E — Evidence (Факт/пример) Подкрепляет тезис конкретным фактом, статистикой или примером For instance, / Specifically, / According to... / Research suggests that... 1–2 предложения
E — Explanation (Объяснение) Объясняет, как доказательство поддерживает тезис This demonstrates that... / This suggests that... / This illustrates... 1–2 предложения
L — Link (Связь) Возвращает к тезису эссе или переходит к следующему абзацу Therefore, / Consequently, / This further supports... / Overall, it is clear that... 1 предложение

Пример полного PEEL-абзаца (тема: Права человека)

P One of the most fundamental human rights violations in the contemporary world is the denial of access to quality education.
E For instance, according to UNESCO data, approximately 244 million children worldwide remain out of school, with the majority concentrated in sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia.
E This demonstrates that despite the ratification of international conventions guaranteeing the right to education, systemic inequalities continue to prevent millions of children from exercising this fundamental right.
L Consequently, the protection of this right requires not merely legislative commitments, but sustained investment in educational infrastructure and targeted policies addressing structural poverty.
❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО ✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО
One problem is education. Many children do not go to school. This is bad. So governments should help. → (Band 4: нет академических маркеров, нет CNP, нет specifics) One critical barrier to human rights is inadequate access to quality education. For instance, UNESCO reports that 244 million children remain out of school globally. This demonstrates that... Consequently,... → (Band 7: PEEL + discourse markers + CNP)
💡 Лайфхак: Каждый Body Paragraph в IELTS Task 2 должен следовать структуре PEEL. Это гарантирует полноту аргумента и помогает examiner легко найти все части — что повышает Coherence & Cohesion.

▶ Тема Б: "Economic inequality is the greatest threat to human rights."

📘 Grammar 4: Academic Language — Discourse Markers в IELTS Task 2

В этом блоке мы изучаем, как discourse markers работают на уровне всего эссе — от введения до заключения. Правильное использование маркеров на каждом уровне текста — залог Band 7+ по Coherence & Cohesion.

Часть эссе Функция Discourse markers для каждой части
Introduction (Введение) Парафраз темы + thesis It is widely debated whether... / In recent years, ... has become a subject of considerable controversy. / This essay will argue that...
Body 1 — открытие Первый аргумент One significant aspect of this issue is... / A primary concern regarding... is... / It is frequently argued that...
Body 1 — переход внутри Добавление деталей Furthermore, / Moreover, / In addition to this, / Specifically,
Body 2 — открытие Противоположный или второй аргумент On the other hand, / Conversely, / However, it could be argued that... / Proponents of... contend that...
Body 2 — уступка Признать другую сторону Admittedly, / While it is true that... / Despite this, / Even though...
Conclusion Обобщение + финальная мысль In conclusion, / To summarise, / Overall, it is evident that... / The evidence suggests that...

Полная карта дискурсивных маркеров для прав человека (Band 7)

Категория Band 5–6 (слабые) Band 7+ (сильные)
Добавление Also, And also Furthermore, Moreover, In addition to this, What is more,
Противопоставление But, However Nevertheless, Conversely, On the other hand, Notwithstanding this,
Причина Because, So Consequently, Therefore, As a direct result of this, Hence,
Уступка Although Admittedly, Despite the fact that, Even though, Whilst acknowledging that,
Иллюстрация For example For instance, To illustrate this point, As evidenced by, A case in point is
Вывод In conclusion To summarise, Overall, it is clear that, The evidence strongly suggests that,
🎓 Academic Upgrade (Band 7): Ключевое правило — в каждом абзаце Task 2 используй минимум 3 разных discourse marker: 1) в topic sentence, 2) при переходе к Evidence, 3) в Link. Например: "One critical issue is... [P]. For instance,... [E]. This demonstrates that... [E]. Consequently,... [L]." — это уже структура Band 7.

📄 Reading 1: The Universal Declaration of Human Rights

Инструкция: Прочитай текст и подчеркни все discourse markers. Классифицируй каждый: добавление / противопоставление / причина / уступка / иллюстрация / вывод. Найди минимум 10 маркеров.

Adopted by the United Nations General Assembly in 1948, the Universal Declaration of Human Rights remains one of the most significant legal documents in human history. The Declaration enshrines thirty fundamental rights and freedoms applicable to all human beings, regardless of nationality, race, gender, or religion. Nevertheless, despite its universal aspirations, the realisation of these rights remains profoundly unequal across the globe. In particular, civil and political rights — such as freedom of expression, the right to a fair trial, and protection from arbitrary detention — continue to be systematically violated in numerous authoritarian states. Furthermore, economic and social rights, including the right to education, healthcare, and an adequate standard of living, remain inaccessible to hundreds of millions of people worldwide. As a result, international human rights organisations have increasingly argued that a more robust and enforceable framework of global accountability is urgently required. Consequently, the debate surrounding the effectiveness of international human rights law has intensified considerably in recent decades. Admittedly, progress has been made in certain regions; however, systemic violations persist on a troubling scale.

📄 Reading 2: Freedom of Speech in the Digital Age

Инструкция: Прочитай текст и выдели структуру PEEL в каждом абзаце. Найди: (P) topic sentence, (E) evidence, (E) explanation, (L) link. Также найди и подчеркни все discourse markers.

Freedom of expression is widely regarded as one of the foundational pillars of democratic governance. It is, furthermore, a prerequisite for the effective exercise of numerous other rights, including political participation, access to information, and the pursuit of justice. However, the proliferation of digital communication platforms has introduced unprecedented challenges to the traditional understanding of free speech. For instance, the rapid spread of harmful misinformation and incitement to violence through social media networks has prompted governments worldwide to introduce increasingly restrictive content regulation policies. This demonstrates that the boundaries of permissible speech are, consequently, becoming a subject of intense political and legal controversy. Admittedly, the protection of vulnerable communities from online harassment and hate speech constitutes a legitimate governmental concern. Nevertheless, critics argue that overly broad restrictions on digital expression risk silencing legitimate political dissent and undermining democratic accountability. As a result, international human rights bodies have called for the adoption of proportionate, transparent, and independently overseen regulation of online content. Overall, it is clear that reconciling freedom of expression with the protection of individual dignity remains one of the most complex human rights challenges of the digital era.
