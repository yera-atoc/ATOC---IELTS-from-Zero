🎓 Урок B20 — финальный урок уровня Intermediate. Здесь мы не изучаем новую грамматику, а систематизируем всё, что ты освоил за уроки B1–B19, и применяем это в полном формате IELTS: Writing Task 1 (описание графика, 150 слов) и Writing Task 2 (академическое эссе, 250 слов). 📘 Grammar 1: Повторение B1–B10 — Времена и структуры

Быстрое повторение ключевых грамматических тем первой половины уровня B. Проверь себя: можешь ли ты мгновенно сформулировать правило и привести академический пример для каждой темы?

Урок Тема Ключевое правило Академический пример
B1 Present Perfect Continuous have/has been + V-ing — действие началось в прошлом и продолжается Urbanisation has been accelerating rapidly over the past two decades.
B2 Past Continuous vs Simple was/were + V-ing (фон) + Past Simple (событие) While governments were implementing reforms, inequality was simultaneously growing.
B3 Future Perfect & Continuous will have + V3 / will be + V-ing — к моменту в будущем By 2050, the global population will have reached approximately ten billion.
B4 Passive Voice (все времена) be + V3 в любом времени; Impersonal: It is argued/believed It is widely reported that carbon emissions have increased significantly.
B5 Third Conditional If + Past Perfect, would/could have + V3 Had governments acted sooner, the environmental crisis could have been averted.
B6 Mixed Conditionals Past→Present: If had+V3, would+V / Present→Past: If were, would have+V3 If stricter policies had been adopted, inequality would not be as severe today.
B7 Reported Speech (продв.) Сдвиг времён + reporting verbs: argue, claim, suggest, assert Researchers argued that the data strongly supported the hypothesis.
B8 Relative Clauses Defining (no commas) vs Non-defining (, which/who,) The policy, which was introduced in 2015, has yielded mixed results.
B9 Gerunds & Infinitives verb + -ing / verb + to+V; некоторые глаголы допускают оба варианта Addressing systemic inequality requires implementing comprehensive reforms.
B10 Modal Verbs (deduction) must/can't/might/should have + V3 — вывод о прошлом The sharp decline in birth rates must have been caused by economic instability.
❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО ✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО
If the government would have acted, the crisis could be avoided. → (смешение типов: would have не используется в if-clause Third Conditional) If the government had acted earlier, the crisis could have been avoided. → (Third Conditional: If + Past Perfect, would/could have + V3)
💡 Лайфхак: При написании IELTS Task 2 старайся использовать минимум 3 разных грамматических структуры в одном эссе. Examiner оценивает Grammatical Range and Accuracy — разнообразие важнее количества.

📝 Задание 1: Быстрая диагностика B1–B10 — исправь ошибку в каждом предложении

▶ The population has been grow significantly since 2000. [B1]

▶ While she studied, the phone rang and interrupted her concentration. [B2 — нужен Past Continuous]

▶ By 2030, renewable energy will replace fossil fuels completely. [B3 — добавь Future Perfect]

▶ The report wrote by international researchers last year. [B4 — нужен Passive]

▶ If they had more funding, they could developed better infrastructure. [B5]

▶ He said that climate change is the biggest threat humanity faces. [B7 — сдвинь время]

📘 Grammar 2: Повторение B11–B19 — Академический язык

Вторая половина уровня B сфокусирована на академических структурах IELTS. Это тот арсенал, который отличает Band 6 от Band 7.

Урок Тема Ключевая структура Пример Band 7
B11 Cause & Effect / Concession due to / owing to / whilst / despite the fact that Despite the fact that investment increased, poverty rates remained high.
B12 Comparatives продвинутый considerably/significantly more... than; the majority of; a minority of A significantly higher proportion of urban residents have access to healthcare.
B13 Advanced Passive / It is... It is argued/believed/suggested/reported/estimated that... It is widely estimated that over one billion people lack access to clean water.
B14 Noun Clauses that-clause / wh-clause как subject/object What remains unclear is whether these policies will prove effective long-term.
B15 Cleft Sentences What...is / It is...that — акцент It is the lack of political will that prevents meaningful reform.
B16 Participle Clauses -ing/-ed clause вместо relative clause Driven by economic necessity, millions of people migrate to urban centres annually.
B17 Hedging may/might/appear to/seem to/arguably/it is possible that Economic inequality arguably represents the most pressing challenge of our era.
B18 Complex Noun Phrases pre-mod + N + of + N + post-mod The unprecedented proliferation of data-driven technologies has transformed society.
B19 Discourse Markers + PEEL Furthermore/Nevertheless/Consequently + P-E-E-L structure Consequently, governments must adopt a comprehensive regulatory framework.

Комбинирование структур B11–B19 в одном предложении

Структуры Комбинированный пример Band 7–8
B13 + B18 It is widely argued that the rapid proliferation of artificial intelligence poses unprecedented socioeconomic challenges.
B15 + B17 It is arguably the absence of robust international regulation that has allowed corporate data exploitation to intensify.
B16 + B11 Driven by demographic pressures, governments have been compelled to reform pension systems, despite considerable political resistance.
B14 + B19 What the evidence consistently demonstrates is that, consequently, inequality cannot be addressed without structural economic reform.
B17 + B12 A considerably larger proportion of the population may be affected by automation than current estimates suggest.
🎓 IELTS подсказка (Band 7+): Ни одна из этих структур сама по себе не гарантирует высокий балл — важна их корректная комбинация. Examiner ищет "a wide range of structures used with full flexibility and accuracy" (Band 8). Цель уровня B: уверенно использовать минимум 5–6 разных структур в одном эссе.

📝 Задание 2: Комбинирование B11–B19 — напиши одно предложение, совместив указанные структуры

▶ B13 (It is believed) + B18 (CNP): тема — изменение климата

▶ B15 (It is...that) + B17 (arguably): тема — образование

▶ B16 (Participle clause) + B11 (despite): тема — миграция

▶ B19 (Discourse marker) + B14 (Noun clause): тема — технологии

▶ Напиши полное предложение, включающее B12 + B13 + B18 одновременно

📘 Grammar 3: Язык IELTS Writing Task 1 — описание графика/диаграммы

IELTS Academic Writing Task 1 требует описания визуальных данных (график, диаграмма, таблица, карта) за минимум 150 слов. Это НЕ эссе — здесь нет personal opinion. Задача: точно, академично и структурированно описать то, что показывают данные.

Часть Task 1 Что писать Языковые средства
Introduction (1–2 предложения) Парафраз задания: что показывает график, за какой период The graph/chart/table illustrates... / The diagram shows... / The data presents...
Overview (2–3 предложения) Главные тенденции без цифр — самое важное что видно Overall, it is clear that... / The most striking feature is... / In general, ...
Body Paragraph 1 (описание деталей) Конкретные данные: цифры, периоды, сравнения In [year], ... stood at / reached / accounted for... / By contrast, ...
Body Paragraph 2 (сравнение/тренд) Дополнительные детали, пиковые/минимальные значения ...rose sharply to / fell dramatically / remained relatively stable at...

Академический словарь для описания трендов

Тренд Глаголы (нейтральные) Глаголы (резкие) Наречия
Рост increase, rise, grow, climb surge, soar, rocket, jump significantly, substantially, dramatically, sharply
Падение decrease, decline, fall, drop plummet, plunge, collapse considerably, markedly, steeply, rapidly
Стабильность remain stable, plateau, level off — relatively, approximately, broadly
Колебание fluctuate, vary oscillate slightly, marginally, moderately
Достижение пика reach a peak/high of, peak at — —
Достижение минимума reach a low/trough of, bottom out at — —

Структура Overview — самая важная часть Task 1

Band 5 Overview Band 7 Overview
The graph shows increases and decreases in different countries. Overall, it is evident that Country A experienced the most dramatic growth throughout the period, whilst Country C consistently recorded the lowest figures.
There are some differences between the data. The most notable trend is the sharp divergence between developed and developing nations, particularly after 2010.
❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО ✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО
In my opinion, the graph shows that the economy is getting worse. → (Task 1 — нельзя давать личное мнение) Overall, the data suggests a consistent downward trend in economic output, particularly between 2015 and 2020. → (объективное описание тренда)
💡 Лайфхак: Overview — это не просто список фактов. Это твой вывод о самом важном паттерне. Examiners say: "Many candidates fail to include an overview, which immediately limits the score to Band 5." Всегда пиши Overview — это быстрый Band 6 минимум.

📝 Задание 3: Task 1 Language — опиши воображаемые данные, используя академический словарь

📄 Reading 1: Global Inequality in the Twenty-First Century

Инструкция: Прочитай текст и выполни два задания: (1) найди и подчеркни все структуры из уровня B (Passive, CNP, discourse markers, hedging, participle clauses); (2) составь краткий план PEEL для каждого абзаца.

Global inequality has been widening at an unprecedented rate over the past three decades, driven largely by the unequal distribution of technological advancement and capital accumulation. It is widely estimated that the wealthiest one percent of the global population now controls more wealth than the remaining ninety-nine percent combined. Furthermore, the proliferation of data-driven economies has arguably deepened structural inequalities by rewarding those who already possess advanced digital skills and significant financial capital. Consequently, a considerable proportion of the global workforce faces the very real prospect of technological displacement without adequate social protection mechanisms. Admittedly, significant progress has been made in reducing extreme poverty, particularly in East and South-East Asia. Nevertheless, what the evidence consistently demonstrates is that economic growth alone is insufficient to guarantee equitable outcomes. It is, therefore, the quality and inclusivity of economic institutions that ultimately determine whether growth translates into broadly shared prosperity. Addressing these deeply entrenched disparities requires not merely financial investment, but a fundamental reconceptualisation of the relationship between technological progress and social equity.

📄 Reading 2: The Future of Work in the Age of Automation

Инструкция: Прочитай текст как IELTS Task 1 "passage" — найди все данные (цифры, проценты, временные маркеры). Затем найди все примеры hedging language (B17) и complex noun phrases (B18).

The automation of labour markets represents one of the most consequential and widely debated economic transformations of the contemporary era. It is estimated that between forty and sixty percent of existing jobs in developed economies contain tasks that are technically automatable using currently available technologies. However, it would be misleading to suggest that automation will simply eliminate employment on a large scale. What appears more likely, based on available evidence, is a fundamental restructuring of occupational categories rather than their wholesale elimination. Routine cognitive and manual tasks seem particularly susceptible to automation, whilst roles requiring complex social interaction, creative problem-solving, and ethical judgement appear considerably more resilient. Nevertheless, the transition period may prove deeply disruptive for workers in affected industries, particularly those with limited educational qualifications or restricted access to retraining programmes. It is therefore crucial that governments invest substantially in the long-term development of adaptive educational frameworks capable of responding dynamically to rapidly evolving labour market demands. The evidence strongly suggests that proactive policy intervention, rather than reactive crisis management, represents the most effective approach to navigating this profound structural transformation.

💡 Лайфхак: В этом тексте — идеальный пример Task 1 Overview: "What appears more likely...is a fundamental restructuring of occupational categories rather than their wholesale elimination." Это — обобщение главной тенденции без цифр. Именно такой язык нужен в Overview раздела Task 1.

✍️ Writing Tasks — B20: Полный формат IELTS Task 1 + Task 2

Итоговые writing задания уровня B. Task 1 — описание графика (150 слов). Task 2 — полное академическое эссе (250 слов). Оба задания выполняются с применением всех структур B1–B19.

Writing Task 1 — Описание линейного графика (150 слов)

📋 Writing Task 1 (минимум 150 слов)

Задание:

The line graph below shows the percentage of the population with access to the internet in four regions (North America, Western Europe, East Asia, Sub-Saharan Africa) between 2000 and 2020.

Воображаемые данные для описания:

North America: 43% (2000) → 91% (2020) — steady rise

Western Europe: 28% (2000) → 87% (2020) — rapid growth, plateau after 2015

East Asia: 9% (2000) → 73% (2020) — dramatic surge after 2008

Sub-Saharan Africa: 1% (2000) → 22% (2020) — slow but consistent growth

Структура ответа:

Introduction (2 предложения): The line graph illustrates... / The data presents...

Overview (2–3 предложения): Overall, it is clear that... Главные тренды без цифр.

Body 1 (4–5 предложений): North America + Western Europe — детали и сравнение

Body 2 (4–5 предложений): East Asia + Sub-Saharan Africa — контраст и тренды

Требования к языку:

• Минимум 6 глаголов тренда (rose, fell, plateaued, surged...)

• Минимум 4 наречия интенсивности (dramatically, consistently, sharply...)

• Минимум 1 структура B13 (It is notable/evident that...)

• БЕЗ личного мнения. БЕЗ Introduction.

🎯 Band Check: 150+ слов · Overview присутствует · Band 7 (Task Achievement + CC)

Writing Task 2 — Полное академическое эссе (250 слов)

📋 Writing Task 2 — ИТОГОВОЕ ЭССЕ УРОВНЯ B (250 слов)

Тема:

"Technological advancement inevitably increases inequality within societies. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"

Полная структура эссе Band 7 (250 слов):

It is widely debated whether... [парафраз темы с CNP из B18]

In conclusion, whilst... [B11 уступка], it is evident that... [thesis paraphrase]
