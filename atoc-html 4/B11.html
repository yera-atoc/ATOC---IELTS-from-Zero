Grammar 1 — Cause & Effect: базовые связки

В уровне A19 ты изучал linking words для причинно-следственных связей. Сейчас мы углубляемся: изучим продвинутые конструкции с различными частями речи и академическими оборотами для IELTS.

💡 Совет:

Связь с уровнем A: В A19 ты изучал because, so, therefore, however. Теперь мы используем более сложные конструкции: due to, as a result of, consequently — с существительными и герундиями, а не только с предложениями.

Причина: союзы и предлоги

Конструкция Часть речи после Пример
because предложение (S+V) Globalisation has accelerated because technology has advanced rapidly.
because of существительное / герундий Trade expanded because of improved transport networks.
due to существительное / герундий Unemployment rose due to the automation of manufacturing.
owing to существительное / герундий Many companies relocated owing to lower labour costs abroad.
as a result of существительное / герундий Living standards improved as a result of economic integration.
since / as предложение (причина) As globalisation intensifies, inequality between nations may widen.

Следствие: союзы и наречия

Конструкция Часть речи после Пример
so предложение Trade barriers fell, so domestic industries faced new competition.
therefore предложение (after ;/.) Global supply chains expanded; therefore, production costs declined.
consequently предложение (after ;/.) Jobs moved overseas; consequently, unemployment rose in some regions.
as a result предложение (after ;/.) Investment increased; as a result, GDP grew significantly.
thus предложение (formal) Wages stagnated, thus widening the wealth gap.
hence существительное / предложение The demand for skilled workers increased, hence the skills shortage.

❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО:

Due to the prices rose, many people... (due to + предложение — ОШИБКА)

✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО:

Due to rising prices, many people... Because prices rose, many people...

📝 IELTS подсказка:

В IELTS Writing избегай повторения because. Чередуй: due to / owing to / since / as — это демонстрирует Range of Vocabulary и повышает Band Score.

Zadanie 1

Выбери правильную конструкцию и закончи предложение:

* Many people migrate ___ (because / due to) better job opportunities abroad.

* The economy grew rapidly; ___ (therefore / due to), living standards improved.

* ___ (Since / Because of) trade barriers were removed, exports increased.

* Income inequality widened ___ (as a result / as a result of) globalisation.

Grammar 2 — Concession: Although / Whilst / Despite / In spite of

Уступительные конструкции (concession) признают противоположный аргумент, но затем возражают. Это ключевой навык для IELTS Task 2 — показывает balanced argument и академический стиль.

Although / Even though / While / Whilst — + предложение

Конструкция Оттенок Пример
Although + S+V нейтральная уступка Although globalisation creates wealth, benefits are not equally distributed.
Even though + S+V более сильная уступка Even though trade has expanded, poverty persists in many regions.
While + S+V контраст / уступка While developed nations benefit, developing countries often struggle.
Whilst + S+V академический вариант while Whilst free trade promotes growth, it can also destabilise local industries.

Despite / In spite of — + существительное или герундий

Конструкция После неё идёт Пример
Despite + noun/gerund существительное Despite rapid economic growth, inequality has increased in many countries.
Despite + noun/gerund герундий Despite signing trade agreements, many nations protect key industries.
In spite of + noun/gerund существительное In spite of global integration, cultural diversity remains strong.
Despite the fact that + S+V предложение Despite the fact that wages have risen, purchasing power has declined.

Although vs Despite

Although + ПРЕДЛОЖЕНИЕ:

Although prices are high, demand remains strong.

Despite + СУЩЕСТВИТЕЛЬНОЕ:

Despite high prices, demand remains strong.

Позиция в предложении

В начале: Although X, Y.

В середине: X, although Y.

Despite всегда в начале придаточного.

❌ НЕПРАВИЛЬНО:

Despite the economy is growing, inequality rises. (Despite + предложение — ОШИБКА)

✅ ПРАВИЛЬНО:

Despite economic growth, inequality is rising. Although the economy is growing, inequality rises.

Zadanie 2

Соедини предложения, используя although / despite / whilst / in spite of:

* Globalisation creates jobs. It also causes unemployment in some sectors.

* Many countries have trade agreements. Protectionism is still common.

* Living standards have improved. The wealth gap continues to grow.

* Technology connects the world. Cultural differences persist.

Grammar 3 — Advanced Cause & Effect: Номинализация и академические фразы

Номинализация (nominalization) — это превращение глаголов и прилагательных в существительные. В академическом письме это создаёт более формальный, сжатый стиль — необходимо для Band 7+.

Номинализация для выражения причины и следствия

Глагол/прилагательное Существительное Академический пример
globalise globalisation The globalisation of trade has transformed national economies.
migrate migration Mass migration has reshaped the demographic profile of many cities.
develop development Economic development in Asia has lifted millions out of poverty.
integrate integration Regional integration has facilitated the free movement of goods.
compete competition Increased competition has driven down consumer prices globally.
expand expansion The expansion of multinational corporations has fuelled economic growth.
distribute distribution Unequal distribution of wealth remains a central challenge.
automate automation Industrial automation has displaced millions of low-skilled workers.

Академические фразы для Cause & Effect

Причина (Cause) Следствие (Effect)
A key factor contributing to X is Y X has led to / given rise to / resulted in Y
X can be attributed to Y X has had a significant impact on Y
One of the main causes of X is Y X has brought about considerable changes in Y
X stems from Y The consequence of X is Y
X is largely driven by Y X has paved the way for Y

💡 Совет:

Вместо 'because prices are high' пиши 'owing to elevated price levels' или 'as a consequence of price increases'. Номинализация делает текст академичным и лаконичным — это именно то, что ищут экзаменаторы IELTS Band 7.

Zadanie 3

Перепиши предложения, используя номинализацию и академические фразы:

* Because technology is advancing, jobs are disappearing. -> The ... of technology has led to...

* People migrate because they want better lives. -> Mass ... can be attributed to...

* Because countries trade more, their economies grow. -> The ... of trade has resulted in...

* Automation changes manufacturing quickly. -> The rapid ... of ... has brought about...

Grammar 4 — Academic Language: Cause & Effect в IELTS Task 2

Academic Upgrade: Как объединить все инструменты — причинно-следственные связки, уступки, номинализацию — в связный абзац IELTS Band 7.

Academic Upgrade: Сравнение стилей письма

Band 5 (слабо) Band 7 (академично) Что улучшилось
Globalisation is good because it makes jobs. The globalisation of trade has generated employment opportunities, particularly in developing economies. Номинализация + specificity
But there are bad effects too. Whilst economic integration offers significant benefits, it simultaneously gives rise to considerable social challenges. Whilst + формальный стиль
Because of globalisation, poor countries get better. As a result of greater economic integration, living standards in emerging markets have improved substantially. as a result of + nominalization
Although globalisation is good, it has problems. Despite the considerable economic advantages of globalisation, its social consequences cannot be overlooked. Despite + noun phrase
So inequality grows. Consequently, income inequality between skilled and unskilled workers has widened considerably. Consequently + детальность

PEEL абзац с Cause & Effect + Concession

Элемент PEEL Пример предложения
P — Point (тезис) One of the most significant consequences of globalisation is the widening of income inequality.
E — Explain (объяснение) As a result of the automation of manufacturing processes, low-skilled workers in developed nations have faced widespread unemployment.
E — Example (пример) In the United States, for instance, the deindustrialisation of the Rust Belt can be largely attributed to the relocation of factories to lower-wage economies.
L — Link (уступка+связь) Whilst this process has undeniably driven down consumer prices, the social costs — including increased inequality and community decline — cannot be disregarded.

📝 IELTS подсказка:

Academic Upgrade: Идеальный абзац IELTS Task 2 содержит: 1 cause/effect connector (due to, consequently), 1 concession (although, whilst, despite), 1 nominalisation, 1 конкретный пример. Это Band 7 formula.

Reading 1 — Глобализация и неравенство

Прочитай текст. Найди и подчеркни все конструкции Cause & Effect и Concession. Запиши: какую функцию выполняет каждая конструкция.

Globalisation has fundamentally reshaped the global economy over recent decades. Due to the removal of trade barriers and advances in communication technology, goods, capital, and labour now move more freely across borders than ever before. As a result, multinational corporations have expanded their operations into emerging markets, consequently driving economic growth in previously underdeveloped regions. However, the benefits of globalisation have not been equally distributed. Whilst countries such as China and South Korea have experienced remarkable economic growth owing to their integration into global supply chains, many developing nations in sub-Saharan Africa remain largely marginalised. Despite signing numerous international trade agreements, these countries often lack the infrastructure and human capital required to compete effectively. Furthermore, even within prosperous nations, income inequality has widened considerably. As a result of automation and the offshoring of manufacturing, low-skilled workers have faced stagnating wages and unemployment. Although overall GDP has risen, the distribution of this wealth has become increasingly unequal, raising fundamental questions about the social sustainability of the current model of globalisation.

Reading 2 — Культурная глобализация

Прочитай текст. Выпиши 6 конструкций Cause/Effect или Concession и переведи их на русский. Объясни функцию каждой.

Cultural globalisation — the spread of ideas, values, and consumer culture across national boundaries — represents one of the most debated consequences of economic integration. Due to the dominance of Western media and entertainment industries, critics argue that local cultures face an existential threat. Consequently, traditional languages, customs, and art forms are at risk of being displaced by a homogenised global culture. Despite these concerns, many scholars argue that cultural exchange can be mutually enriching rather than simply destructive. Whilst the influence of global brands and social media platforms is undeniable, local cultures have demonstrated remarkable resilience. As a result of globalisation, hybrid cultural forms have emerged, blending indigenous traditions with international influences in innovative ways. Furthermore, although the dominance of English as a global lingua franca may disadvantage speakers of minority languages, it has simultaneously enabled greater cross-cultural communication. Owing to digital technology, previously isolated communities can now share their culture with a global audience, giving rise to a more diverse and interconnected cultural landscape than critics of globalisation often acknowledge.

💡 Совет:

В IELTS Reading важно понимать связи между идеями. Когда видишь despite / although — жди контраст. Когда видишь consequently / as a result — следует вывод. Эти маркеры помогут правильно ответить на вопросы о мнении автора.

Writing Tasks — B11

В этом уроке Writing Task 2 — 190 слов. Тема: Глобализация. Используй Cause & Effect конструкции, Concession и номинализацию.

Writing Task 1 — Paragraph Practice

Задание:

Напиши 1 абзац (70–80 слов) о влиянии глобализации на культуру своей страны. Используй минимум 3 разные конструкции из этого урока.

Требования:

* Используй хотя бы 1 конструкцию Cause (due to / owing to / as a result of)

* Используй хотя бы 1 конструкцию Concession (although / despite / whilst)

* Используй хотя бы 1 номинализацию

* Band Check: 3 разные конструкции + логичность = Band 6

Writing Task 2 — IELTS Essay (190 слов)

Тема эссе:

"Globalisation has had more negative effects than positive ones on developing countries. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"

Структура (190 слов):

* Introduction (2 предл.): парафраз + твоя позиция

* Body 1 (4–5 предл.): аргументы о негативных эффектах + пример

* Body 2 (4–5 предл.): контраргумент — позитивные эффекты + пример

* Conclusion (2 предл.): paraphrase + balanced final thought

Используй обязательно:

* 2+ конструкции Cause & Effect (due to, consequently, as a result of...)

* 2+ конструкции Concession (although, despite, whilst, in spite of...)

* Минимум 2 номинализации (globalisation, migration, automation...)

* PEEL структура в обоих абзацах Body

Band Check: Cause/Effect + Concession + Nominalization + PEEL = Band 6-7
