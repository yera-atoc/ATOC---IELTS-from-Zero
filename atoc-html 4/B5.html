[G] Grammar 1 — Third Conditional: структура и значение

Third Conditional — это условие в прошлом, которое уже НЕ случилось. Мы говорим о воображаемом результате невозможного сценария. В политике — это анализ «что было бы, если бы...».

Часть Структура Пример
IF-CLAUSE (условие) If + Past Perfect (had + V3) If the government had invested more...
MAIN CLAUSE (результат) would have + V3 ...the economy would have grown.
Отрицание в IF If + had NOT + V3 If they had not signed the treaty...
Отрицание в MAIN would NOT have + V3 ...the war would not have ended.
Вопрос Would + S + have + V3 Would the crisis have been avoided?

[!] Важно: порядок частей свободный. Можно начинать с If-clause или с Main clause — смысл не меняется. Запятая нужна только когда If-clause стоит ПЕРВЫМ.

Порядок Пример Запятая?
IF → MAIN If they had acted sooner, the damage would have been minimal. [OK] Да
MAIN → IF The damage would have been minimal if they had acted sooner. [X] Нет
[X] НЕПРАВИЛЬНО [OK] ПРАВИЛЬНО
If they would have acted, it was better. (ОШИБКА: would в If-clause недопустимо!) If they had acted sooner, the result would have been different. (Past Perfect в if-clause + would have + V3)
[!] ЛАЙФХАК: Запомни формулу 3-3: три слова в if-clause → had + V3; три слова в main clause → would have + V3. Эта симметрия помогает избежать смешения с другими conditionals.
[I] IELTS: В IELTS Writing Task 2 Third Conditional используется для анализа прошлых политических решений: 'Had the authorities implemented stricter regulations, the financial crisis would have been averted.' Это сразу показывает Band 6+.
[T] ЗАДАНИЕ 1: Составь Third Conditional из подсказок
> government / not raise taxes → economy / grow faster
> president / sign the agreement → relations / improve
> parliament / pass the law / earlier → citizens / benefit more
> leaders / not ignore the warnings → disaster / be prevented
> Переведи свои предложения на русский и объясни значение каждого

[G] Grammar 2 — Could / Might have + V3: возможность в прошлом

Could have + V3 и Might have + V3 — это модальные конструкции для описания нереализованных возможностей в прошлом. В политическом дискурсе они выражают критику, сожаление и альтернативные сценарии.

Конструкция Значение Пример
could have + V3 Возможность, которая была, но не реализована The minister could have resigned earlier.
might have + V3 Предположение о прошлом (слабее) The crisis might have been avoided.
should have + V3 Совет / критика — нужно было сделать They should have consulted the experts.
would have + V3 Гипотетический результат (Third Cond.) It would have succeeded with more funding.
couldn't have + V3 Невозможность в прошлом They couldn't have known the outcome.
[OK] could have = была возможность [OK] might have = неуверенное предположение
The UN could have intervened earlier. The vote might have changed the outcome.
Politicians could have found a compromise. More funding might have saved the project.
We could have prevented the recession. The treaty might have reduced tensions.
[X] НЕПРАВИЛЬНО [OK] ПРАВИЛЬНО
They could have to act differently. (ОШИБКА: после could have только V3, не 'to + V1'!) They could have acted differently. (could have + V3 — третья форма глагола)
[!] ЛАЙФХАК: Чтобы не путать could have / might have / should have, запомни эмоцию: • could have = упущенная возможность (нейтрально) • might have = неуверенность о прошлом • should have = критика / сожаление (эмоционально)
[T] ЗАДАНИЕ 2: Выбери правильную конструкцию и объясни выбор
> The ambassador _____ (could/might/should) have refused to sign — it was clearly wrong.
> Nobody knows for sure, but the rebels _____ have had outside support.
> The prime minister _____ have consulted Parliament before making that decision.
> Without the strike, the factory _____ have continued operating for years.
> Придумай 2 собственных предложения о политическом событии прошлого

[G] Grammar 3 — Had... инверсия: академический стиль

Инверсия с Had — это формальная альтернатива Third Conditional. Вместо "If + had" мы переносим Had в начало предложения. Это типичная конструкция академического письма и политической риторики. Band 7+ в IELTS Writing.

Обычная форма Инверсия с Had Значение
If the policy had been stricter, Had the policy been stricter, …налоги были бы выше
If they had not intervened, Had they not intervened, …кризис продолжился бы
If he had won the election, Had he won the election, …страна изменилась бы
If the law had passed earlier, Had the law passed earlier, …последствий удалось бы избежать

[K] Правило: Убираем "If", переставляем Had перед подлежащим. Остальная часть предложения (main clause) НЕ меняется. Запятая после инверсионного блока обязательна.

Компонент Структура Пример
Инвертированный блок Had + S + V3/been + ... Had the president acted swiftly,
Результат S + would have + V3 ...the crisis would have been averted.
Отрицание Had + S + not + V3 Had they not signed the accord,
С been Had + S + been + прилагательное Had the economy been stronger,
[X] НЕПРАВИЛЬНО [OK] ПРАВИЛЬНО
Had the government would act differently... (ОШИБКА: would не используется в инверсионном блоке!) Had the government acted differently, the outcome would have been positive. (Had + S + V3, ...would have + V3)
[!] ЛАЙФХАК: Инверсия с Had — это «знак качества» в IELTS Writing. Если ты используешь хотя бы одну такую конструкцию в Task 2, это автоматически повышает оценку за Grammatical Range. Начни с простой замены: вместо 'If leaders had cooperated' напиши 'Had leaders cooperated'.
[I] IELTS: Пример Band 7 фразы: 'Had the international community responded more decisively, the humanitarian crisis would arguably have been less severe.' — здесь совмещены инверсия + hedging (arguably). Это мощная комбинация для высокого балла.
[T] ЗАДАНИЕ 3: Перепиши предложения в форму инверсии
> If the treaty had been signed in time, hundreds of lives would have been saved.
> If the minister had not resigned, the situation would have been different.
> If both parties had agreed, the referendum would have been unnecessary.
> If the UN had intervened, the war would have ended sooner.
> Составь 2 собственных предложения с инверсией на тему политики

[G] Grammar 4 — Academic Language: Unless / Even if / Only if в условных предложениях

Продвинутые условные связки Unless, Even if, Only if придают академическому тексту точность и убедительность. В IELTS они сигнализируют о высоком уровне языковой компетенции. Рассмотрим, как их комбинировать с Third Conditional.

Связка Значение Пример с 3rd Conditional
Unless Если только не / за исключением Unless they had intervened, the conflict would have escalated.
Even if Даже если бы Even if the bill had passed, the problem would have remained.
Only if Только если (строгое условие) Only if both parties had agreed could a deal have been reached.
As long as При условии что As long as talks had continued, peace would have been possible.
Provided that При условии что (формальный) Provided that funding had been secured, the project would have succeeded.
[B] РАЗГОВОРНЫЙ стиль [P] АКАДЕМИЧЕСКИЙ стиль (Band 6–7)
If they hadn't done it, it would have been bad. Had they not intervened, the outcome would have been catastrophic.
If she had known, she might have helped. Even if she had been informed, the outcome might have differed.
I think it could have been different. One could argue that the situation would arguably have been different.
[!] ЛАЙФХАК: Only if + инверсия создаёт «двойную инверсию»: Only if both leaders had agreed could a peace agreement have been reached. Это конструкция уровня Band 7–8. Используй её в заключении эссе для максимального эффекта.
[I] IELTS: ACADEMIC UPGRADE: В IELTS Task 2 при обсуждении политических решений чередуй конструкции для разнообразия: 'Had stricter policies been implemented (инверсия), the economic downturn would have been less severe. Even if some measures had faced opposition (Even if), their long-term benefits would arguably have outweighed the costs (hedging).' — Band 7 комбинация.
[T] ЗАДАНИЕ 4: Academic Writing — используй продвинутые связки
> Перепиши: 'If they hadn't signed, it would have been worse.' → Unless...
> Перепиши: 'Even if they had tried, it would have failed.' → Only if...
> Напиши 1 абзац (3–4 предложения) о политическом решении прошлого, используя:
> → Had... (инверсия) + could/might have + V3 + unless/even if
> Самопроверка: нашёл ли ты все три элемента в своём абзаце?

[R] Reading 1 — Political Decision-Making

Прочитай текст и подчеркни все конструкции Third Conditional, Could/Might have + V3 и инверсии с Had. Найди не менее четырёх таких конструкций.

The 2008 global financial crisis remains a subject of intense academic debate. Had regulators imposed stricter oversight on investment banks in the years preceding the collapse, the scale of economic damage might have been significantly reduced. Critics argue that policymakers could have identified the warning signs earlier, as several economists had published detailed analyses of the housing market instability. Moreover, had international financial institutions coordinated their responses more effectively, the contagion spreading to emerging economies would arguably have been contained. Even if some economic contraction had been inevitable, the social consequences — rising unemployment, austerity measures, and political instability — would have been less severe. The crisis demonstrated that political will, rather than technical knowledge, was the limiting factor. Only if governments had been willing to challenge powerful financial interests could meaningful reforms have been implemented before the collapse. This lesson remains relevant for contemporary policymakers navigating complex economic challenges.

[R] Reading 2 — Diplomacy and Missed Opportunities

Прочитай текст. Найди и выпиши: а) все примеры Could/Might have; б) все примеры инверсии с Had; в) выражения с Unless или Even if.

The failure of the 1994 Rwandan peace process stands as one of the most examined examples of diplomatic failure in modern history. Had UN peacekeeping forces been granted the authority to act, the genocide might have been prevented or at least substantially curtailed. General Dallaire, the UN commander on the ground, repeatedly requested permission to intervene — permission that could have saved countless lives. Even if complete prevention had proved impossible, an earlier and more decisive international response would have reduced the death toll dramatically. Analysts suggest that had key member states prioritised humanitarian obligations over political considerations, the trajectory of events would arguably have been altered. Unless international institutions undergo fundamental reform in their decision-making procedures, similar tragedies might recur. The case illustrates how political inaction, even when solutions existed, can produce irreversible consequences — consequences that no subsequent policy could have fully addressed.
